14 Best Airbnbs for Bachelorette Parties in 2024

So you’re planning your bachelorette party but have absolutely no clue where to start. First up is the location. Popular choices include the likes of Nashville, New York City, and Austin, but of course, choose wherever is geographically convenient for you and your dearest friends and wherever you think you’ll have the most fun. Next is where you’ll stay. A bachelorette Airbnb is definitely the way to go because it offers more space and privacy, but it’s also likely more wallet-friendly than, let’s say, a hotel suite or multiple rooms.

What you’ll want to pay close attention to when searching for the best bachelorette Airbnb is the space itself (how many bedrooms and bathrooms does it have?), its capacity (can it fit all of your besties?) and its amenities (are there fun activities to do on the property?). This might sound like a lot to consider all at once, especially when you’re thinking about so many other bridal duties and wedding to-do list items. However, we’re not here to leave your mind in a jumbled mess. Behold, the 14 best Airbnbs for a bachelorette party.

From sunny Los Angeles to party-central New York City, you’re bound to find an Airbnb on this list to book for you and your BFFs. Your bachelorette party is meant to be a fun and love-filled, celebratory time, and the homes we pinpointed make for the perfect spaces to do all this with your ride-or-dies. Remember that Airbnbs can get booked up in the blink of an eye, so get to it!

Grand Ole Opry HoF House

  • Price: $237/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 8 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.98 stars (168 reviews)

Stay in an Airbnb that’s only a mile from the historic Grand Ole Opry and 10 minutes from downtown Nashville. This home has all of the modern touches and appliances you could ask for. Plus, it has an impressive 4.98-star rating for an affordable $236 a night. 

The Waylon

  • Price: $401/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 5.5 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 12 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.98 stars (101 reviews)

This spacious home is named after the iconic singer and songwriter, Waylon Jennings. It doesn’t get more Nashville than that. This stunning abode is great for entertaining a large group, but it still has a hint of country charm. Be sure to check out the Airbnb’s pool table and rooftop patio. 

Luxe Townhome

  • Price: $1,425/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 8 guests
  • Superhost: No
  • Rating: New

Valued at a whopping $20,000,000, this modern luxury townhome sits right above the Upper West Side. Its most notable features include high ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows, a patio, a roof deck, and skyline views. If you’re an interior design snob, your jaw will be on the floor as you explore the home and its works of art, sculptural furniture, and light, calming colors.

Apartment in Chelsea

  • Price: $445/night
  • Space: 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 11 guests
  • Superhost: No
  • Rating: 5.0 stars (8 reviews)

In search of a bachelorette Airbnb in NYC that has a more central location and costs less than $1,000? Book this beautiful apartment in Chelsea for $445 a night. Two bedrooms have two queen-size beds each, while the third has a full-size bed and a twin daybed. Six beds is plenty of space for you and your besties. The apartment is also a one-minute walk from the 1 train, making getting places in the city such a breeze.

Private 2-Acre Home

  • Price: $608/night
  • Space: 6 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 16+ guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 5.0 (26 reviews)

If space is what you want, space is what you’ll get with this private two-acre house. You certainly won’t run out of things to do in this Airbnb thanks to the pool table, ping pong table, foosball table, pickleball court (!!), basketball hoop, hot tub, fire pit, soccer field, mini golf area, and pool. You’ll be surrounded by grass and trees, so if you’re aiming for a more secluded feel, this home is it. 

Grand Oasis

  • Price: $798/night
  • Space: 3 bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 8 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.90 stars (48 reviews)

This open-concept Airbnb has light shining throughout the entire house. Outside, you’ll notice a backyard, deck, outdoor kitchen, and pool. But if you’re in need of some good eateries, shopping, and entertainment, you can easily walk to Ladybird Lake, Downtown Austin, Rainey Street, and East 6th Street.

Casa Marbella 

  • Price: $722/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 14 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 5.0 stars (110 reviews) 

My draw dropped when I saw the pool and the pillars on this gorgeous Airbnb. If all you want to do is lay out in the sun with your closest friends, there’s no better place to do that than in this 3,500-square-foot home. A gym, bar, pool, fully-equipped kitchen, roomy living room, and 82-inch smart TV are just the beginning of what makes this Airbnb a must-book property.

Casa Coco

  • Price: $505/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 14 guests
  • Superhost: No
  • Rating: 4.46 stars (24 reviews) 

This hidden gem features a newly renovated kitchen, a large yard, outdoor dining, and much more. I can’t forget to mention the 200-year-old oak tree in the back, along with the hanging daybed and solar lanterns. This Spanish-style beauty can fit up to 14 guests in its four bedrooms and four bathrooms, which is plenty of space for your bachelorette party.

The Lighthouse: A Luxurious Mountain Haven Estate

  • Price: $752/night
  • Space: 5 bedrooms, 4.5 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 12 guests
  • Superhost: No
  • Rating: 5.0 (4 reviews)

Everyone’s booking bachelorette Airbnbs in warmer climates, but maybe you’re looking for something not so traditional. If your dream bachelorette party consists of snuggling by the fireplace with hot cocoas and a charcuterie board, accompanied by some quality time on the ski trails, allow me to introduce you to Park City, Utah. It’s the perfect spot for a winter wonderland escape with your best friends, and this luxurious estate will be sure to keep you cozy and entertained. The 2.75-acre lot offers an outdoor jacuzzi, spacious patios, and an observatory. Who knows, you might even spot an elk or two!

Smile High Haven

  • Price: $2,209/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 12 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 5.0 (2 reviews)

If you’re centering your trip around hitting the slopes, this new ski-in/ski-out home is your best option. I can say that with confidence because it literally has a private ski tower with lockers and an elevator so that you can access the mountains with ease. As for what you get with the Smile High Haven, just a rooftop patio with a fireplace and hot tub, a community hot tub, and a ski lounge.

Resort Style Vegas Oasis

  • Price: $641/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 8 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.77 stars (176 reviews)

If you’re celebrating in Las Vegas, you’re probably going to want a place close to the Las Vegas Strip, but still has a bit of distance and privacy. This Airbnb is minutes away from all the action yet still feels like its own haven. There’s plenty to do inside the Airbnb—cue the grotto with a spa and swim-up bar, covered patio, outdoor BBQ area, billiard table, and multi-game arcade machine.

Luxury Private Estate

  • Price: $571/night
  • Space: 6 bedrooms, 4.5 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 14 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.59 stars (49 reviews)

This Airbnb’s interior design is absolutely everything (the wishbone chairs and woven pendant lights have my heart). Everything in this space feels so intentional, and this homey feel is certainly important when you’re about to spend a weekend with your best gal pals. The backyard, however, is where all the bells and whistles come out, like the pool, hot tub, sauna, and grassy area. 

Luxe Resort Home 

  • Price: $615/night
  • Space: 5 bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 8 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 5.0 stars (50 reviews)

Spend a weekend living like a celeb in this beautiful house. Its biggest selling point is its picturesque view of the city, which you can even enjoy poolside. Not to mention, the home includes a ping pong table, theater/game room, and balconies in four of the rooms. There’s plenty to do on this property, but you won’t be a far drive from popular neighborhoods such as Hollywood, Malibu, Santa Monica, and Universal City (where Universal Studios is located). 

The Steel Estate 

  • Price: $1,299/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms
  • Capacity: 8 guests
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.82 stars (49 reviews)

Get a taste of the Hollywood Hills in The Steel Estate, a four-bedroom and four-bathroom home that’s tucked away in one of the most star-studded neighborhoods in Los Angeles. Take advantage of the large deck, multi-level backyard, outdoor tubs, sunset views, and more.
