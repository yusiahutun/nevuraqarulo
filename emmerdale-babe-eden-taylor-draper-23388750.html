Emmerdale babe Eden Taylor-Draper looks very different to Belle Dingle in glam snaps

Emmerdale bombshell Eden Taylor-Draper is best known for her role as Belle Dingle, who she has been playing since 2005.

Whilst fans are used to see Belle wearing casual clothes to fit in with her village life, Eden looks totally different from the youngest member of the Dingle family.

The blonde beauty looked seriously glamorous in one of her latest pictures, which saw her celebrate turning 23 at the end of last year.

Wearing a plunging long-sleeved beige top which was tucked into figure-hugging black jeans, Eden dressed up for the special occasion.

Clinking glasses with an unseen person behind the camera, Eden looked very sophisticated as she showed off a full face of make-up, including pink lipstick, and her blonde locks in sleek curls.

The star's social media page is also full of loved-up snaps with her partner Ed, who she has been with for nearly five years.

One picture showed the pair smiled for a picture together on Ed's graduation day in Durham last year, looking happier than ever.

Eden looked gorgeous in a loose pink floral patterned playsuit with a drawstring neckline and a nipped in waist.

Smiling at the camera with her arm around her partner, the soap star looked seriously elegant in the loved-up picture, which she simply captioned: "You did it," adding hearts to the post.

A third picture showed the pair outside the cathedral on the special day as they smiled at someone off camera.

Still with her arm around her newly graduated beau, Eden showed off a glimpse of her long legs in the tiny playsuit, which she added a blazer to.

Whilst Eden looks very different from Belle, she has made several references to her Emmerdale family on her social media.

Sharing a picture of a polaroid from her co-star Isabel Hodgins' birthday last year, the gang all looked stunning while out for a meal.

The snap showed Fiona Wade, Roxy Shahidi, Laura Norton, Charley Webb, as well as Eden and Isabel as they gathered around a booth.

Eden looked stunning as she opted for a leopard print blouse with long sleeves and a collared neckline.

Isabel went for a similar option, wowing her fans in a high-necked black top with a leopard print patterned over the black top and sheer sleeves.

The gang all looked like they were having a great time to mark Isabel's birthday, as they looked a lot more glamorous than their village alter-egos.

Sadly, they won't have been able to meet up as a group recently due to the pandemic, which has seen the soap pause filming again.
