1. Andrea Mitchell

  • Full name: Andrea Mitchell
  • Date of birth: 30 October 1946
  • Age: 76 years old (as of 2023)

Andrea Mitchell is a television journalist, anchor, and commentator for NBC News. She works as the NBC News Chief Foreign Affairs & Senior Washington Correspondent, a position she has held since 1994.

Since 2008, she has hosted Andrea Mitchell Reports, a news and commentary show on MSNBC. Before hosting her programme, Andrea Mitchell was an anchor under the MSNBC Live umbrella.

Andrea Mitchell joined NBC News in July 1978. She initially served as a general correspondent and later served as the NBC News energy correspondent.

Her portfolio includes working as the Chief White House Correspondent and Chief Congressional Correspondent for NBC News. In 2019, she received a Lifetime Achievement Emmy award for her journalistic work.
