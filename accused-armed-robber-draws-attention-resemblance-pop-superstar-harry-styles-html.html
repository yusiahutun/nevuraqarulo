The mug shot of an Altoona, Pennsylvania man accused of multiple crimes - including armed robbery and making terroristic threats - was getting attention Tuesday, as the suspect bore a resemblance to pop star Harry Styles.

Jason Sanderlin, 21, is accused on felony counts of aggravated assault, robbery, conspiracy and criminal trespass; and misdemeanor counts of making terroristic threats, false imprisonment and indecent assault, police said, according to WTAJ.

In court docs, state police said Sanderlin used an automatic rifle to force three people on the ground at an apartment in Tyrone, Pennsylvania on January 21 - after he kicked down the door to the dwelling.
