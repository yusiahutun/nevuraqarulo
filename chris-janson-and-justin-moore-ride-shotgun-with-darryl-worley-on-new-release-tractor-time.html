Chris Janson And Justin Moore Ride Shotgun With Darryl Worley on New Release Tractor Time

February 21, 2024
blog

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess)—Platinum-selling country artist Darryl Worley has teamed up with fellow country stars Chris Janson and Justin Moore to release a brand-new track celebrating the timeless charm of rural life. Titled “Tractor Time,” the song, penned by Worley alongside Phil O’Donnell, captures the essence of finding solace and joy in driving circles in a field after a long day’s work.

“My new song, ‘Tractor Time,’ drops today, and I think it’s the perfect summer anthem,” explains Worley. “I’m so grateful to have Justin Moore and Chris Janson on this tune with me. It’s a fun little number with a big fat groove and a contagious melody. Turn up the dial and get yourself a little dose of redneck therapy.”

The new track is available now via The Valory Music Co. It offers listeners a blend of infectious melodies and heartfelt lyrics, inviting fans to immerse themselves in the nostalgia of country living. Worley, Janson, and Moore deliver a harmonious performance that will resonate with fans of all ages. You can listen to the new track HERE.

“I have always had a ton of respect for Darryl Worley,” shares Moore. “He’s a great writer, a great country singer and a great artist overall. Most importantly, though, he’s a great human being. I’m honored to be on a song with him, as I’ve been a fan for a long time.”

Janson echoes Moore’s sentiment. “I’ve always had a great respect for Darryl. He’s a great guy and a great American,” he says. “I’m very proud that he asked me to join him on ‘Tractor Time.'”

Beyond his musical achievements, Worley is known for his philanthropic endeavors, including his work with The Darryl Worley Foundation, annual charity events, and unwavering support for the United States military and veterans. With nearly 20 hit singles to his name, including chart-toppers like “Awful, Beautiful Life,” “Have You Forgotten?,” and “I Miss My Friend,” Worley continues to make his mark on the country music scene while also giving back to his community and country.
