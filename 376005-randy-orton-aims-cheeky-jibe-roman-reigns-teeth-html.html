Randy Orton aims cheeky jibe at Roman Reigns for getting new teeth

March 11, 2024
blog

- Randy Orton and Roman Reigns briefiy traded jibes on social media on Monday, August 24

- The tweef came from the fact that Reigns made a public appearance with a new set of teeth

- The Big Dog has been away from the wrestling scene since March when the pandemic hit the world

WWE superstars Randy Orton and Roman Reigns recently engaged in an entertaining online 'wrestling match' and it all stemmed from the latter's new set of teeth.

Reigns made a surprise return to Summerslam on Monday, August 24, and while fans were thrilled to see him back, others could not help but notice something different in his appearance.

The Big Dog, as Reigns is usually referred to, has been out of action since March and this has been largely due to the pandemic.

During this time, he managed to get in some new dental veneer; something which sent fans into an online frenzy of banter.

Among the people who took to social media to ridicule Reigns was his rival Orton who used the popular character Brian from the TV show Family Guy, to aim a dig at the wrestler.

"Was really nice seeing #thebigdogromanreigns is back," Orton wrote on Instagram.

While the post might have been Orton’s cheeky way of welcoming back Reigns, The Big Dog did not take it very lightly and also took to his own Instagram on Monday to fire back.

In his post, he subtly suggested Orton was fading from the wrestling scene.

“@randyorton, If was you, I’d be talking about me too, because no one is talking about you,” he hit back.

Family first

Reigns also explained why he chose to stay away from wrestling since the pandemic started and it all went down to trying to stay safe for his family.

"The decision was taken mainly because each performer travels so much, and we are all such a diverse group and from all over the place. I'm not convinced, and I can't trust the fact that everybody is taking it as seriously and locking themselves down at home like I am. I trust my life with my co-workers every time I step foot in the ring, but I just can't put the same trust when it has my children, my wife and my family involved,” he told The Hindu.

There was a bit of mystery on why Reigns decided not to work Wrestlemania36, and he recently released a video divulging his absence.

It turns out Reigns and his wife are expecting a pair of twins, hence the need for the 34-year old to focus a bit more on family rather than his wrestling career.
