More On Diesel Brothers

It is true indeed that life changes after meeting with someone who you are destined to be with for a long time. It is quite an interesting meeting the Diesel Brothers, originally David Sparks and David Kiley, now better known by names of “Heavy D” and “Diesel Dave.”

Both of them are stars of the hit reality TV show Diesel Brothers on the Velocity Channel, which have grown to be a hit since its debut in early 2016.

Alongside the Sparks Motors crew, the dynamic duo of Heavy D and Diesel Dave have brainstormed as well as built some of the craziest and most amazing yet beautiful diesel vehicles seen in the world. They also major in educating millions about what it takes to turn these trucks into outstanding creations and also have fun on the sets.

Heavy D said of their first meeting:-

We met at a church for a single people gathering. We were supposed to go there and find girls, and we found each other. It was a match made in heaven.

Chiming on his co-star's words, Dave revealed that they had met a decade ago.
