Paris Hilton Sparkles in a Mint-Green Gown with Daring Chest Cutouts

March 23, 2024
blog

Paris Hilton brought the glamor to last night's Billboard Latin Music Awards ceremony.

The heiress arrived to the event—which took place at the Watsco Center in Coral Gables, Florida—wearing a spectacular bedazzled gown from Area. The mint-green piece vertical cutouts on the bodice, with the cutouts stretching from the neckline to her waist. The sweeping tulip maxi skirt also featured a thigh-high leg slit, which helped to show off her pair of sparkling silver pumps with a metallic toe cap. Hilton accessorized with more sparkling silver elements, including a set of crystal embellished arm cuffs, diamond stud earrings, and a glittery rectangular clutch.

As for her glam, she opted for a dramatic smoky eye accented by pink blush and a glossy nude lip. She swept her blonde hair up into an elegant bun, with a few face-framing pieces styled down and into a sleek side-part.

During the ceremony, Hilton joined Danilo Carrera to present an award to Valentina Bueno and Karol G.

Billboard//Getty Images

Christopher Polk//Getty Images

Lately, the mogul has had a busy, fashion-filled month.

She made several stylish appearances during Paris Fashion Week. During Chanel’s Spring/Summer 2024 runway show, for instance, Hilton sat front row while channeling Legally Blonde's Elle Woods in a black skirt suit trimmed with fuzzy hot-pink fur on the hemline, waist, and torso. The set included a zip-up vest and a glossy satin skirt adorned with a bedazzled Chanel logo belt. The day before, she showed up to the Miu Miu show in a preppy look that consisted of a belted black minidress, Mary Jane platform pumps, a rectangular purse, sunglasses, and a headband.
