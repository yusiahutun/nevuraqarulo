Kendall Jenner's Off-Duty Look Includes Mom Jeans and Clogs

May 07, 2024
blog

Kendall Jenner just stepped out in one of the year's hottest and most unexpected trends: clunky clogs.

The supermodel was seen running errands in Los Angeles yesterday, wearing a relaxed and timeless off-duty look consisting of a black cropped T-shirt and baggy dark jeans cinched with a black leather belt with gold hardware. She finished the ensemble with black clogs with large gold buckles, a matching leather shoulder bag, skinny rectangular sunglasses, and small gold hoop earrings.

She left her long hair down and seemed to wear a natural makeup look.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

In the past few weeks, Jenner has been giving us a master class in styling minimalistic staples.

Last week, the 818 founder was seen walking around L.A. in the most perfect autumn transition piece, a cropped beige crochet tank, which she paired with white mom jeans, her classic black leather belt, and glossy black square-toe ballerina flats. She accessorized with her go-to rectangular sunglasses and small gold hoop earrings.

Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Also that week, Jenner was photographed in a nearly all-black outfit composed of a tiny black bandeau top that showed off a subtle amount of underboob. She paired the piece with a black silk wrap skirt from The Row, which featured a cobalt blue gradient toward the hem for an electric pop of color.

And last month, Jenner wore her favorite sunglasses and leather belt with white high-waisted straight-leg trousers, a salmon-colored cropped cardigan from Miaou, and the prettiest white square-toe flats.
