Title: Freelance Writer

Education: University of Pennsylvania, Goldsmiths, University of London

Location: New York, NY

Lauren K. Tappan is a freelance writer with several years of experience in the magazine industry. At InStyle, she covers beauty and fashion, helping readers to discover the best products on the market.

Highlights:

  • Harper's Bazaar: Freelance Fashion Editor
  • ELLE Magazine: Freelance Fashion and Beauty Editor
  • Town and Country Magazine: Assistant and Writer
  • Into The Gloss (Glossier): Freelance Beauty Writer
  • Philadelphia Style Magazine: Editorial Intern
  • The Walk Magazine (University of Pennsylvania): Editor-in-Chief

Experience

Lauren's enthusiasm for fashion and beauty writing began in college, while writing for The University of Pennsylvania's fashion magazine and interning for Philadelphia Style Magazine.

After college, she worked as a freelance beauty writer for Into The Gloss and shortly after landed a position at Town & Country Magazine. As the assistant to the editor-in-chief of T&C, Lauren wrote articles for T&C's digital site, specifically developing a portfolio of fashion and beauty content.

Currently, Lauren works as a freelance editor, identifying trends and curating shopping guides for a variety of publications including InStyle, Harper’s Bazaar, and ELLE Magazine.

Education

Lauren graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a bachelor's degree in communications and journalism, and she also pursued media studies at Goldsmiths, University of London.
