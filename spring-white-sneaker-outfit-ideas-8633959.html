5 Unfussy Styles I'm Wearing on Repeat With White Sneakers This Spring

August 31, 2024
blog

I own eight pairs of white sneakers, and that’s after parting with four others. IMO, there’s nothing more foolproof than a staple pair of white kicks; they’re comfortable, seasonaless, and simple to style. However, that doesn’t mean they make you immune to fashion ruts — so, take a peek at the below white sneaker outfit combos I can’t get enough of this spring, including jeans, dresses, and skirts.   

Levi’s Wedgie Straight Jean

Don’t sleep on the magic of jeans just because they’re basic — especially a well-fitting straight-leg pair. I own the Levi’s Wedgie Jean and love how it skims my thighs, lifts my behind, and cuts off at the ankle. The Madewell ‘90s pair mimics the style of the previous option with its retro design, while NYDJ and Gloria Vanderbilt offer stretchier versions for max comfort. Roll your jeans up to show off your shoes or let the pants fall over the vamp for a laid-back approach; it’s a match made in sneaker heaven.  

J.Crew Gwyneth Slip Dress

Dress your kicks up with a slip dress, like J.Crew’s Gwyneth find. The gown is available in eight colors, all of which graze the calf, boast adjustable straps, and feature a scoop neckline. The Nordstrom V-neck version offers a sexier take on the trend, while effortlessly pairing with white tennis shoes. For spring-perfect alternatives, snag both the BP. and Cantonwalker floral-print dresses.     

Big Dart Oversized Button-Down Shirt

IMO, there’s nothing more chic than an oversized button-up shirt and clean white sneakers. Big Dart offers a $26 pick that includes cuffed sleeves, a poplin collar, and 22 hues, like pink, green, and tan. Lean into pinstripes for a nautical allure, consider linen for something breezy, or snag a short-sleeve design for warmer days.   

Hidden Jeans Back-Slit Denim Midi Pencil Skirt

I’ve been pretty open about having a thing for versatile spring denim skirts. Options like the Hidden Jeans back-slit skirt and Petal and Pup midi skirt are the perfect counterpart to white shoes thanks to their ageless, flattering, and timeless appeal. Madewell, and J.Crew also demonstrate just how fun (and practical) a front slit can be.

Caslon Stripe Linen-Blend Blazer

Blazers make my comfortable kicks look more professional when going into the office and add structure and poise to my look. Linen blazers are especially noteworthy for spring; they keep you cool while looking sophisticated. The Caslon striped version is my favorite find, though you can’t pass up a breathable $43 option from Amazon. 

While each style looks great on their own with white kicks, the separates look even better when you mix and match them; just think of how cute an oversized button-up top would be with a denim midi skirt! So, scroll back through, secure your favorite picks, and get ready to create some model spring ensembles.
