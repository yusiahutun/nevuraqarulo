Who is Bad Bunny?

Bad Bunny was born on March 10, 1994, in Amirante Sur, Puerto Rico. His birth name is Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio. He is 29 years old (as of March 2023). His parents are Tito Martínez, a truck driver, and Lysaurie Ocasio, a retired schoolteacher.

Bad Bunny grew up alongside his two brothers, Bernie Martinez Ocasio and Bysael Martinez Ocasio. He debuted his music career at the age of 14. Bad Bunny rose to prominence in 2016 when he released the song Diles. The biggest breakthrough in his career came in 2018 when he featured on Cardi B's song I Like It alongside J Balvin.

He has released several singles that have topped musical charts. These include Soy Peor, Mia, Bakiti, and others. Bad Bunny has also pursued a wrestling career. He is famous for his appearances on WWE and WrestleMania. Bunny is also a one-time WWE 24/7 Champion.
