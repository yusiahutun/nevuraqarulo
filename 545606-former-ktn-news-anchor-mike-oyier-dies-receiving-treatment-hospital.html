Former KTN News Anchor Mike Oyier Dies While Receiving Treatment in Hospital

February 15, 2024
blog
  • Veteran and celebrated TV journalist Mike Oyier has died while undergoing treatment at the Nairobi Hospital
  • The scribe was known for his mastery of the king's language and how he dazzled in his exciting interviews
  • Media personalities and Kenyans at large mourned the late journalist while recalling his contribution to the media space

Renowned TV journalist and former KTN news anchor Mike Oyier has died, shocking many on social media.

The celebrated journalist who controlled the airwaves with his impeccable mastery of the king's language died while undergoing treatment at Nairobi Hospital.

It is still unclear what was ailing the vibrant journalist and for how long, even as tributes from scribes and netizens have been pouring online.

Kenyans mourn Mike Oyier

Veteran radio host Maina Kageni took to his social media to mourn Oyier, referring to him as a legend and one of the best newscasters Kenya has ever seen.

Rita Tinina dies at her Kileleshwa home

Another Kenyan journalist whose death shocked many was NTV reporter Rita Tinina, who was found dead in her Kileleshwa home on March 17.

Preliminary reports indicate she was supposed to be on duty at NTV, where she worked, but failed to report, prompting managers to go to the house where her lifeless body was found.

A post-mortem report later unveiled that the celebrated journalist died of severe pneumonia.
