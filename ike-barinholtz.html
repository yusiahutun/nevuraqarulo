Isaac Barinholtz (born February 18, 1977) is an American actor, comedian, writer, director, and producer. He is best known for his role as a cast member on the comedy series MADtv (2002–2007), Eastbound & Down (2012), and The Mindy Project (2012–2017). Barinholtz has appeared in films such as Neighbors (2014) and its 2016 sequel, Sisters (2015), Suicide Squad (2016), Blockers (2018), and Late Night (2019). He also co-wrote Central Intelligence (2016), and directed, wrote, produced, and starred in The Oath (2018).
