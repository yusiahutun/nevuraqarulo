How to Watch, Plus Episode Schedule

April 09, 2024
blog

One of the most highly-anticipated new shows of 2023 is finally here, and fans can hardly contain their excitement.

Daisy Jones & The Six became a New York Times best-seller when it was released in 2019, selling more than a million copies. Now, the Reese Witherspoon-produced adaptation of the Taylor Jenkins Reid novel is heading to the small screen.

Here's everything to know about this hit, which first airs on March 3.

Where to watch and stream Daisy Jones & The Six:

Lacey Terrell/Prime Video

Daisy Jones & The Six streams exclusively on Prime Video, so you'll need an Amazon Prime membership to watch. If you haven't had an Amazon Prime membership in the past 12 months, you can sign up for a free 30-day trial.

Who is starring in Daisy Jones & The Six?

Lacey Terrell/Prime Video

The incredible cast will likely feature some new faces, including Riley Keough, Sam Claflin, Camila Morrone, Will Harrison, Suki Waterhouse, Josh Whitehouse, Sebastian Chacon, Nabiyah Be, Tom Wright and Timothy Olyphant.

When do new episodes of Daisy Jones & the Six come out?

This one is going to keep you waiting from week to week! The 10 episodes will be released on Fridays in March, but you'll get to watch more than one at a time.

Here's the release schedule so you can mark your calendar:

March 3:

  • Episode 1, "Come and Get It"
  • Episode 2, "I'll Take You There"
  • Episode 3, "Someone Saved My Life Tonight"

March 10:

  • Episode 4, "I Saw the Light"
  • Episode 5, "Fire"
  • Episode 6, "Whatever Gets You Through the Night"

March 17:

  • Episode 7, "She's Gone"
  • Episode 8, "Looks Like We Made It"

March 24:

  • Episode 9, "Feels Like the First Time"
  • Episode 10, "Rock 'n' Roll Suicide"

Is Daisy Jones & The Six based on a book?

Yes! It's based on the popular novel by the same name by best-selling author Taylor Jenkins Reid. If you're a "must read the book before you watch the show" kind of person, we highly recommend the audiobook version. It's read by a full cast and captures the documentary style of the novel.
