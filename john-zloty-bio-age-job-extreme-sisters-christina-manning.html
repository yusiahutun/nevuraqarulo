Extreme Sisters: Are John Zloty And Christina Manning Still Together?

Yes, John Zloty and Christina Manning are still together. In fact, they have been married since 14 August 2021, two months after the season 1 finale aired.

The most recent posts of the two together on John’s IG were in the first week of January 2023. John posted on his Instagram, “Congratulations to my beautiful Wife on the release of the teaser trailer for her show #extremesisters Check out the premiere of Season 2 on Jan 23rd! #tlc.”

As for Christina’s IG, it was on 15 January. She posted the same pic with the caption, “Mom & Dad.”

But, the return of Christina and Jessica is the only news in 2023, the show also explores Christina and John’s pregnancy journey. Jessica Dunagan, the twin sister of John and Christina, was shown arguing in the teaser over how much she was involved in the pregnancy. When John claimed that Jessica did not need to go to Christina’s ultrasound appointment, the married couple got into an argument.

“John wants my sister all to himself,” Jessica complained. Before the season premiere, Christina – who has two kids from her first marriage – reflected on her pregnancy via Instagram on January 7.

“I thought I was done having kids; almost 40, blessed with two already. I will say, being pregnant this time felt different to me,” she wrote alongside several photos of her baby bump. “I embraced it with more gratitude. I realized that this may be the last time I would get to have this experience again and I knew how quickly time goes. How fast my babies grow up. How the chaos was really an amazing experience looking back.”

“I was able to live in the moment and be more patient, more present, and more grateful. Wow, I was going to love another little human just as much as I already love my two,” she continued. “It was an embodied spiritual moment and I am forever grateful.” Christina added that she was grateful to experience her third pregnancy with “the most amazing man.”

“I didn’t have a Dad growing up, and so I know how blessed my children are to have amazing male role models in their life. One thing I have got right in this life is that I have chosen really great men to have children with,” the reality star concluded. “I got it right with Maddox and Maelyn’s Dad and I have won the jackpot with John. Feeling so so blessed and had to share.”

The earliest post of John and Christina together dates back to December 2020.

Jessica’s involvement in the birth of Christina’s child is only natural for the twins, especially because this is the last pregnancy Christina intends to have.

“I’m gonna be right there next to her, pulling the baby out, putting the baby on her chest,” Jessica said. “Like, I am fully immersing into this power of womanhood.”

The upcoming birth of Christina is one of the main plotlines for Christina and Jessica in Season 2 of Extreme Sisters. By the conclusion of the season, she will probably give birth, but she hasn’t revealed the date of her delivery on social media just yet. She posted a selection of images from her pregnancy on Instagram in January 2023 and made comments about being 40 years old and pregnant.

Therefore, it seems likely that her child was born not too long before that post. In the video, Christina says she wants to give birth at home in water “all natural.” She then enters her bathtub to experience it for herself. She also discloses her initial desire to give birth in the woods, but John vetoed that.
