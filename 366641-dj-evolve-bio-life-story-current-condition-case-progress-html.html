DJ Evolve current condition

What is the current condition of the DJ? DJ Evolve has been undergoing treatment for gunshot wounds after the incident at B-Club in Nairobi in January. He has been recuperating at the Nairobi Hospital, whereby he spent a considerable amount while at the Intensive Care Unit.

Although there were earlier reports that DJ Evolve had been released in mid-May, it turned out that he was moved to the general ward. It wasn't until June 18, 2020, that the DJ was released from the hospital and is currently under home care.
