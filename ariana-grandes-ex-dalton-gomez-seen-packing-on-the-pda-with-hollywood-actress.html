Ariana Grande's ex Dalton Gomez seen 'packing on the PDA' with Hollywood actress

August 11, 2024
blog

Not long after they filed for divorce, Ariana Grande’s ex-husband, Dalton Gomez has reportedly been seen kissing actress Maika Monroe.

The music star started speculation she’d split with Gomez this summer, as she was spotted a number of times in public without her wedding ring. Last month, it was revealed the two had officially filed for divorce, with the date of separation marked all the way back to February 2023. As Ariana is rumored to have moved on with her Wicked co-star, it looks like Dalton is now doing the same.

Dalton Gomez reportedly ‘seen kissing’ Maika Monroe

Although Gomez was a stranger to celebrity life before meeting Ariana, it seems like he can’t keep things low-key anymore, as onlookers saw what looks like the 28-year-old ‘kissing’ the 30-year-old It Follows star.

The photo was first posted on Instagram by celeb gossip page, DeuxMoi. In the text underneath the picture, a source who sent the picture claimed the two were “PACKING on the PDA at Jumbos.”

“They were very friendly, and both introduced themselves,” the source continued. “They would look around to see if anyone was looking then continue to make out in front of everyone.”

Ariana, on the other hand, is rumored to have moved on with her Wicked castmate, Ethan Slater, as they’ve spent time in the UK together, filming the upcoming musical blockbuster.

Rumors began to circulate in July of this year, less than a year after Slater, and his former wife, Lilly Jay, welcomed their first child.

Shortly after rumors surfaced in July, it was reported by PEOPLE that the Spongebob Squarepants Broadway star filed for divorce from his wife.

Neither Ariana Grande nor Ethan Slater have publically commented or confirmed their relationship. However, her new tattoo may be ‘linked’ to her ‘new love.’

Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez reportedly ‘on good terms’

Shortly after the separation between the two was reported, the Thank U, Next singer removed her wedding photos with Dalton from Instagram. However, one ‘blurry’ picture of the couple under the starlight, remains.

Despite the divorce, it’s reported there are no hard feelings between the couple. At the time of the pair officially filing for divorce, sources told TMZ: “They’ve been really caring and respectful of one another every step of this process.”

The publication also reported earlier this week that Dalton will receive $1,250,000, tax-free, from his former wife. The realtor will allegedly receive the money upfront, so there will be no spousal support.

Again, sources told TMZ that there is a “mutual love and respect” between the two.

“There is still a strong mutual love and respect between Ariana and Dalton, and they wanted to make sure everything was done right in lockstep with one another,” insiders claimed.
