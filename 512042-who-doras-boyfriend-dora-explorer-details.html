Who is Dora?

Dora Marquez is an adorable and courageous 7-year-old little Latina female. In each episode, Dora and her best friend, Boots, a monkey, embark on numerous adventures in the wilderness. While exploring, they rely on various valuable and fun tools stored in Dora's crimson backpack.

Who is Dora's boyfriend?

The idea of Dora’s boyfriend is from the overactive imaginations of viewers of this series. Dora is a little girl, but her close relationship with some male characters, especially Diego, has led to speculations that she might be dating.

Are Dora and Diego dating? Well, the producers of the cartoon series confirmed that Dora has no boyfriend. Diego, whom many believe is Dora's boyfriend, is her cousin.

Are Dora and Diego cousins?

Is Diego Dora's boyfriend and cousin? Diego Marquez is Dora’s cousin - though it’s not confirmed how they relate. They’re presumably first paternal cousins considering they have a similar last name.

Diego Marquez is an 8-year-old Latina who is a creature enthusiast. He can talk to animals and rescues them from danger.

Diego first debuted in the 2nd episode of the 3rd season in October 2003, titled Meet Diego. Dora introduced him to his best friend Boots and viewers as her cousin. His mission was protecting animals and safeguarding the environment.

What is the relationship between Pablo and Dora?

Pablo (theoretical name: Pablo Nelson Chaves Ballester) is the first male anime character that appeared on Dora the Explorer. He was introduced in the episode Pablo's Flute.

His grandpa raised him on a farm, and he gifted him a magical flute. Dora and her best friend, Boots met Pablo after he lost his magical flute in the thick forest – they helped him search for the flute. Pablo is affectionate toward Dora in the TV show, making people think he's in love with her.
