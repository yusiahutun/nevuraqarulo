FIFA 17 TOTY Defenders and Goalkeeper LIVE for FUT Ultimate Team on PS4, Xbox One and PC

July 27, 2024
blog

FIFA 17 unveiled the first 11 man TOTY (Team of the Year) at the start of the week and now EA Sports FIFA account has just tweeted to let FUT (FIFA Ultimate Team) fans know that the games TOTY Defenders and Goalkeeper are now available.

The FIFA 17 FUT team of the year is made up of the 11 best players of 2016, as voted for by players peers.

Though many players were nominated, including the likes of Jamie Vardy, the final 11 were revealed at an awards ceremony in Zurich and failed to include a single Premier League player.

The four defenders included in the TOTY were Real Madrid pair Sergio Ramos and Marcelo alongside Barcelona defenders Gerard Pique and Dani Alves.

Bayern Munich's Manuel Neuer picked up the price as the TOTY Goalkeeper.

The official EA Sports FIFA Twitter account also mentioned that TOTY midfielders and forwards are no longer available in packs.

This includes in form cards for Cristiano Ronaldo, Messi, Luis Suarez, Andres Iniesta, Luka Modric and Toni Kroos.
