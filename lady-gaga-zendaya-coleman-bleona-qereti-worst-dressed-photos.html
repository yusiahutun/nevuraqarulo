What the Heck Are They Wearing?!?

October 03, 2024
blog

A blow-up blunder, a trashy throwback and an over-exposed Bravo-lebrity -- the stars are rocking all the wrong trends this week!

Lady Gaga raised some eyebrows when she stepped out in Paris in a plastic inflatable dress, featuring a dozen or so holographic spikes. She balanced out the crazy getup with licorice-hued lipstick and a banged 'do.

The "Applause" singer is no stranger to making a statement with her style, but this dress just leaves us, well, deflated.

Zendaya Coleman missed the mark at the American Music Awards on Sunday, paying homage to the late Aaliyah in a brushed metallic Georgine Spring trench coat worn over matching palazzo pants. She paired the 70's-inspired suit with a mint-green leather bandeau top and Gucci heels.

Between the Diana Ross curls, massive hoop earrings and her nose ring, the pretty pop star looks like she's trying too hard to reinvent her look.

Meanwhile, "Euros of Hollywood" star Bleona Qereti took to the AMAs red carpet wearing, well, not much at all. The Albanian singer bared her bod in a completely see-through dress, a jewel-encrusted thong and silver nipple covers.

We couldn't help but think back to Rose McGowan's naked dress at the 1998 MTV Video Music Awards, but Bleona told toofab that she was not her inspiration.

"I have no idea who she is! I just saw her face in the press next to mine yesterday for the first time in my life," she told us of McGowan. "This must have all been when I was in Europe."

Well, there goes that theory!
