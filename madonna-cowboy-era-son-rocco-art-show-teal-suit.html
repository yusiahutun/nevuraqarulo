Madonna Is Back in Her Cowboy Era

January 31, 2024
blog

Madonna looked like a work of art herself while attending son Rocco Ritchie’s art show Thursday night.

The Queen of Pop made an appearance at the exhibition, titled “Pack a Punch,” in the Miami Arts District. It was a big night for 23-year-old Rocco, as it was his first-ever solo show following his graduation from Central Saint Martins and the Royal Drawing School in London. And Madonna sure marked the importance of the moment with her look: an elegant teal three-piece suit from Roberto Cavalli.

The “Material Girl” singer pulled the set from the Pre-Fall ’24 collection, designed by Fausto Puglisi. Her eye-popping ensemble included a blazer, flared trousers, and a waistcoat, which were all constructed with a jacquard fabric and printed with a bandanna pattern.

Besides the paisley pattern, the music legend further embraced the ongoing cowboy trend by donning a large ivory cowboy hat, which she got from Ruslan Baginskiy (giving us major flashbacks to her Music era). To match the headwear, she wore a pair of white fishnet gloves that were adorned with polka dots, and a pair of pointy heels, also in white. For accessories, Madonna layered thick silver rings over her gloves, and she stacked chunky silver necklaces across her chest, including one that had a giant pendant of the New York Yankees logo. She also carried a black and white handbag.

At the art show, the Grammy winner was joined by four of her other children, David Banda, 18, Mercy James, 18, and twins Stella and Estere, 11. The only one missing was Madonna’s eldest, the cool Lourdes, 27.
