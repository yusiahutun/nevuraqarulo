A throwback video has emerged of veteran actress Jackie Matubia talking about her fears to her ex-fiance Blessing Lung'aho.

In the video, the ex-lovers answered questions about their personal lives by sharing what they envisioned before getting into a relationship.

Why Jackie Matubia was afraid of dating Blessing

In the video, Matubia claimed how disappointed she would be should Blessing leave her with another child after her previous marriage did not work out.

Blessing shared how Matubia did not believe that their relationship could work out and kept wondering if it was possible for them to date.

"It was because I am from another marriage with a child and then I get into another marriage and then it fails. I'll be finished, I am doomed. I was so scared of trusting again," she said.

Then Blessing reminded her that they were still together a year and a few months on, even as they continued to unpack the fears.

Matubia on Blessing being distance lover

At one point, Blessing asked Matubia what would make her feel closer to him, and she paused before reminding him to find a balance between him and his work.

"I just got a job that is taking a lot of 'us' time. When I get an acting role, I normally take some time to become the person I want to play. And that takes away some time," said Blessing.
