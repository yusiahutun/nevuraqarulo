David Harbour violating crucial date rule with Lily Allen worked in his favor

David Harbour and Lily Allen may not have had a meet-cute story from old tales of Hollywood – but it’s just as adorable. However, the Stranger Things star actually broke a vital date rule, which we’re glad to know didn’t ruin his chances with the singer.

David Harbour and Lily Allen’s somewhat normal meeting eventually led to walking down the aisle. The celebrity couple recently celebrated three years of blissful marriage. In adorable photos they shared on Instagram, the duo have clearly enjoyed every second of it so far. But, the movie star did admit how their third date played out – and what should have been a red flag didn’t appear to alarm Lily.

David Harbour and Lily Allen meet on dating app

Can it get any more 21st century than that? It’s hard to believe that Hollywood stars look for love through swiping, but it turns out they do. They’re just like us.

When music star Lily spoke about their connection on The Jonathan Ross Show, she divulged all to the chat show host. She revealed the pair began talking on Raya, the exclusive dating app. It’s only for celebrities, big stars and anyone else who can pass the membership process. In some cases, stars have to be recommended or vouched for by a fellow celeb – so don’t try to download it, as you may be rejected.

Funnily enough, Lily admitted she didn’t even know who David was – but she saw his profile and liked it.

She explained: “I thought he was just like a sexy policeman from a reality TV show because he was wearing a policeman’s uniform. It was a still from Stranger Things. I’d never seen Stranger Things.”

Not only that, but the F You hitmaker said she was just swiping idly on holiday, for “a bit of fun”.

He was in town filming Black Widow, and Lily feared it “wasn’t going to go anywhere”. Likely because the TV star would head back to the US when filming wrapped up. But it did go somewhere.

David’s date mistake

Recalling the early days of the blossoming romance after the first meet between David Harbour and Lily Allen, he spoke about how he thought she was “unbelievable”. They instantly hit it off, but he may have overshared on his third date – which some dating experts suggest to hold off before doing so.

He was explaining that he was in an odd phase but went with the vibe.

He told People: “I was just in this phase where I was like, ‘I’m just going to be brutally honest about everything,’ because why lie? And I told her something about my life, about my beliefs.

“It would take a really extraordinary person to be accepting of the things that I said.

“And I remember thinking: Wow, that’s somebody I want to be around.”

David didn’t explain what things he said, which would need someone “extraordinary” to accept. However, we’re glad that person turned out to be Lily.

‘Just like Titanic’

What is more adorable is how Lily explained what she felt when she first set eyes on him. She said attraction was “pretty instant” between the pair.

The mom-of-two said: “He was sitting underneath a clock. It was a bit like that moment in Titanic, where [Jack] turns around under the clock.”

The couple opted for a Las Vegas wedding, with her two daughters being present. But why did he feel it was time to get hitched? David explained during a family bike ride with the foursome, Lily’s youngest daughter tripped up on what to call him. Feeling confused on naming him ‘David’, ‘Dad’, or something else, the actor wanted to marry Lily “because of the emotional fallout”.

In September 2020 they tied the knot in the casino hotspot, with an Elvis Presley impersonator officiating the special moment. They then enjoyed burgers and fries from a fast food chain for the reception.
