Iris Avenue, Goodge Street

Iris Avenue

Iris Avenues offering of luxe-looking lashes a compliment scorer to say the least; think fluttery, lengthened extensions that never look too heavy or too false. What's more, this up-and-coming spot also offers a range of manicures, facials and massages which can all be served with a delightful cocktail or soft drink. A beauty salon with a truly welcoming, all-in pampered atmosphere.

Iris Avenue, 32 Store St, London WC1E 7BS

Edy London, Fitzrovia

Edy London

Just seconds from Oxford Circus within Beauty Club London you'll find Edyta Kurowska (otherwise known as Edy London). The lash extention extraordinaire has been the trusted celebrity choice for years, counting Trinny Woodall, Lily Allen and Ellie Goulding as clients. If you're new to the world of eyelash extensions or have been searching for the perfect look, Edyta Kurowska specialises in the most natural-seeming feathery lashes which she personalises to your individual eye shape, making them undetectable to the untrained eye.

Beauty Club London, 28 Market Place, Fitzrovia London W1W 8AW

The Clinic at Holland Park

Courtesy of Kendrick PR

When it opened The Clinic at Holland Park set a new standard for treatment destinations. The decor takes away the cold and stale atmosphere that hung about clinics of old, and replaces it with a luxurious members club aesthetic. An afternoon spent here not only offers the opportunity for lash extensions with revered lash technician Jin Kim, but a complete menu of aesthetic tweakments, facials, and surgical treatments.

The Clinic Holland Park, 142-144 Holland Park Avenue, London, W11 4UE

Daxita Vaghela

Courtesy of ClareFord

The beauty industry's not-so-best-kept secret, Daxita Vaghela has continued to be the go-to lash technician for many a magazine beauty director. Known as the 'London lash queen' to her loyal clients, you can find Vaghela located in her clinic at Atherton Cox salon, where she provides a bespoke lash lifting and extension services designed to enhance your features.

Atherton Cox, 18 New Cavendish St, London W1G 8UR

BBB London (Blink Brow Bar)

Courtesy of Blink Brow Bar

With salons dotted across the UK, BBB London (also known as Blink Brow Bar) has become a much-loved and trusted name in the lash world. Don't be deceived by the name; BBB offers much more than just eyebrow treatments. Whether you're looking for a natural but defined lash tint or carefully chosen extensions, their highly-skilled technicians work deftly and precisely to give you your perfect finish.

BBB London, locations throughout London

Pre-

courtesy

Pre- salon in Islington is one of Bazaar’s favourite one-stop-shops for beauty services in the capital, and our editor-in-chief is a loyal customer. As well as facials, nails, body and brow treatments they offer lash services too.

Try a tint to achieve better definition and visible length to your natural lashes, or a LVL lift to see both of those things plus long-lasting curl.

Harrods Hair and Beauty Salon

Julian Broad

Harrods' revamped Hair and Beauty salon houses a fairground of beauty treatments, from ABH Signature Brow treatments to semi-permanent make-up application. A classic set of lash extensions can be applied in as quickly as an hour by a team of meticulously trained technicians, or book in for a YUMI lash perm to open up your eyes with a natural finish.

Shavata Singh Brows and Lashes at Harvey Nichols Beauty Lounge

courtesy

Famed brow guru Shavata Singh also offers excellent lash treatments at her studios dotted around the country, including at the fabulous Harvey Nichols Beauty Lounge.

Here, lash tinting and semi-permanent extension treatments are expertly executed using the highest quality products.

Browfique

Courtesy

Tucked away in Eccleston Yard a five-minute walk from Victoria station, is Browfique (formerly Brow Bar London). Here, the brilliant lash technicians will talk you through every step of the lash process - from the bonding glue to the tint so that you get the lash effect you desire. Their LVL is both dramatic and yet still natural - and makes applying mascara a dream.
