James Franco cannot help himself.

Just when the world thought James Franco may have learnt his lesson when it comes to Instagram, the 36-year-old has posted one of his most shocking pictures yet.

On Thursday night, the actor posted yet another selfie, this time of him standing in front of the mirror in his bathroom in just his boxer briefs with his hand down his pants.

Put your clothes on: Franco posted a near naked photo of himself on Instagram on Thursday before later removing it

Despite already having an Instagram related scandal last month involving a teenage girl, the Spring Breakers star seems determined to kick off May in the same way.

Clearly James - who claims to be addicted to selfies and social media - is doing some sort of social experiment, however, this time even he knew he had gone too far.

Just hours after putting up the image - in which he exposes his pubic hair and almost his nether region - James took it down.

IIt was certainly too much Franco for for Mistresses star Alyssa Milano who took to Twitter with the hilarious comment 'My eyes! My eyes! Make the burning stop. #JustSawTheFrancoSelfie,' which no doubt sent her nearly 2.8 million followers racing to find the image.

'I was waiting for you!': James Franco posted yet another racy selfie to Instagram on Saturday

The image comes as the star once again addressed an incident last month when the actor found himself in hot water after attempting to pick up a young fan via Instagram.

He later apologised but on Wednesday, James told Howard Stern on his radio show that he had been a gentleman.

James said: 'Here's the thing, the way that the news wants to do it... they make it like, I'm perusing young woman. I'm not going to high schools looking for dates. I'm leaving my work and they're coming there.

'I was a gentleman, I said, ''Do you have a boyfriend?,'' and her response was, ''Not when you're around,'' So that to me sounds like, OK, she's interested.'

'BED SELFIE WITH KEEGAN ALLEN!!!!! TIME TO GO NUTS!!!!' James and Keegan Allen took their budding bromance to the next level last month as they posed together for a sexy shirtless 'bed selfie'

The star said it was not the teenager's fault it became a scandal.

'Here's what I'll say in her defense - I don't think she posted it knowing what was gonna happen. I was like anybody, just trying to meet somebody. It's just that my dirty s**t gets put out internationally.'

Despite this, James has continued to post selfies including one where he appeared to be naked in bed but has a sheet draped across him last week.

Taking inspiration from sexting, James captioned the picture: 'WTF HAPPENED? I was waiting for you!!!!!'

He has also enlisted pal Keegan Allen for another 'bed selfie' in which the two men snuggled up under a blanket.

He's clearly moved on from the 'embarrassment': Despite being busted earlier last month for trying to arrange a hookup with a young fan via Instagram, that hasn't stopped the 36-year-old from his cheeky antics, regularly posting selfies from his bed in recent weeks

The This Is The End star talked about his fascination taking pictures like this telling AP they were a 'kind of intimate portrait' of himself and to get people talking.

'It's not like I'm exposing myself or anything,' he said in the interview on Thursday.

He said when he takes pictures of himself 'it's almost like it's connected to you' and that by putting 'that intimate space out there it's kind of this new thing that we're all getting used to'.

The director wrote in a New York Times essay last December titled The Meanings Of The Selfie that he has 'become increasingly addicted to Instagram' and acknowledged that he has 'been accused of posting too many of them'.
