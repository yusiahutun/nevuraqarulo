Blac Chyna praised for emotional conversation with Wendy Williams - 'we should all take note'

August 24, 2024
blog

Wendy Williams and Blac Chyna sat down for an emotional chat in the new lifetime documentary, Where Is Wendy Williams? which focuses on the star’s life following the end of her talk show.

Recently, it was announced that the former TV talk show host had been diagnosed with primary progressive aphasia and frontotemporal dementia, the same form of dementia as actor Bruce Willis.

Wendy Williams and Blac Chyna get deep

Williams and Chyna actually grew close from her talk show, in 2020, as Rob Kardashian’s ex came on to “set the record straight.”

In the chat, the 59-year-old told Blac Chyna, real name Angela White: “We talk about you a lot on Hot Topics, you’re very controversial,” to which the mom of two replied, “Thank you.” The two grew close since that day, show in the emotional conversation in the doc.

“You’ve always been like, honest with me, and put me in my place, in the most motherly, kind way,” Chyna said, after telling Wendy she’d changed her life. “Even when I was going through my darkest times, you never used that against me,” the 35-year-old revealed.

Williams engaged in conversation with Angela without one of her wigs. She then emotionally opened up about her husband, as Chyna said she’d ‘always be there for her.’

The two then said they loved each other before emotionally breaking down in tears whilst holding each other.

Fans ‘respect’ Blac Chyna for the conversation

Upon seeing the emotional conversation between the two, fans took to X, formerly known as Twitter to praise the star for how she sat and listened to Wendy.

“I respect the h*** out of Blac for this. This is true humanity and a truly good soul. I am grateful for people like her. We should all take note,” wrote one.

“All I ever knew about Blac Chyna was what I read in the tabloids. It was so beautiful to see what a kind, genuine and beautiful person she really is,” penned another.

A third said: “Blac Chyna is so real for this man. Nobody really reaches back for Wendy but she did.”
