Andrew Garfield and Florence Pugh's adorable matching outfits have fans 'screaming'

April 11, 2024
blog

Andrew Garfield and Florence Pugh attended Valentino’s spring/summer 2024 show in Paris, and their coordinated pink outfits have us fangirling to the max.

The Hollywood actors are set to star together in the upcoming romance movie, We Live in Time, although it looks like they took their outfit inspo from another hit Blockbuster…

Andrew Garfield and Florence Pugh match in adorable pink outfits

Of course, it’s not the first time Pugh has stunned in pink. Back in 2022, she wore a sheer pink Valentino dress at the Valentino Haute Couture Fall/Winter 22/23 fashion show in Rome.

The Oppenheimer star stunned in a baby pink double-breasted blazer and matching wide-leg trousers. Underneath she wore a chic bralette in the shape of a bow as her bright blonde pixie was spiked up to add a bit of an edge, and she worked it.

Also joining in on the Barbie trend was Pugh’s co-star and ex Spider-Man, Andrew Garfield who wore a bright pink floor length coat. Although he didn’t go full-on Barbie, the coat complimented the star’s orange blouse and beige trousers.

At the time, she received quite a lot of controversy for her outfit choice, and in an open letter on Instagram at the time, penned:

“Listen, I knew when I wore that incredible Valentino dress that there was no way there wouldn’t be a commentary on it. Whether it be negative or positive, we all knew what we were doing.

“I was excited to wear it, not a wink of me was nervous. I wasn’t before, during or even now after.”

She then went on to talk about body positivity, stating: “What’s been interesting to watch and witness is just how easy it is for men to totally destroy a woman’s body, publicly, proudly, for everyone to see.”

Fans go wild for the ‘iconic duo’

As the picture of the two goes viral on X, formerly known as Twitter, fans are doing what they do best, fangirling.

“The newest iconic duo we didn’t know we needed,” penned one.

“This would be a power couple,” wrote another.

“Screaming, crying!!!” exclaimed one excited fan.

One poetic fan said: “It’s annoying how gorgeous they both are. Andrew speaks in poetry and Florence sings like an Angel.”
