Prince Harry feels 'humiliated' as 'enemies laughing' at cruel rumors

August 08, 2024
blog

Prince Harry is apparently “sickened” at comments made by a former Vanity Fair editor who accused Meghan Markle of using him for fame and wealth.

A source has claimed Prince Harry is furious about comments that were made about his marriage by Graydon Carter, a journalist, who previously edited Vanity Fair. Carter suggested that his union with Meghan Markle should be measured in “years not decades.”

“The perceived narrative” about Harry and Meghan’s marriage

A source told Heat Magazine the royal is “upset” by rumors he and his wife are getting a divorce.

“When Graydon Carter weighs in on a topic like this, important people in the highest places listen,” they said, “It’s going to have a domino effect as far as the perceived narrative about their marriage and its long-term prospects goes.”

They added what the 74-year-old said could lead people to believe that he has “inside knowledge about any tension in their relationship” and that “their enemies inside The Firm will be laughing.” However, they were clear that the divorce rumor was “categorically untrue.”

Harry, who recently had a “strict warning” from his dad, is “incredibly sensitive” about the comments made by Carter.

“It’s humiliating for them and divorce is absolutely not an option – they have kids, and despite everything, they really do love each other.”

What did Graydon Carter say about Meghan and Harry?

In an interview with The Sunday Times, he claimed that Harry and his former TV actress wife would not last and that she was merely using him for “money and a title.”

“I would measure the duration of the marriage in years rather than decades. I think she has run rings around poor Harry and gotten what she wanted: notoriety, money, and a title. His usefulness to her diminishes daily.”

He also had something to say about her acting career, which she teased recently. He said: “If there is a Real Housewives of Montecito show in the works, she’d be a shoo-in.”

A nickname for Lilibet

Hollywood actor Tyler Perry recently spoke about his close relationship with Harry and Meghan and said that he has a sweet nickname for their young daughter Lilibet.

He calls her “Little Lili” and says that the two-year-old is “beautiful.”

The celebrity said: “They make beautiful babies – that’s all I’ll say. They make some beautiful kids. Archie and Lili are beautiful.”
