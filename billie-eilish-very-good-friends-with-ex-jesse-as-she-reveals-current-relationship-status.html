Billie Eilish 'very good friends' with ex Jesse as she reveals current relationship status

June 04, 2024
blog

Billie Eilish has opened up to her fans as she reveals her current relationship status, and where she stands with her ex-boyfriend Jesse Rutherford, three months after their split.

With a massive 110 million Instagram followers, we’re sure Billie was bombarded with questions when she posted her question-and-answer box to her stories, but she’s provided fans with everything they want to know.

Billie Eilish on where she stands with ex Jesse Rutherford

Of course, questions about the ex are going to come flooding in at times like this, as one fan simply wrote: Jesse???”

The music star simply answered by writing: “Very very good friends only. My homie forever.”

The Neighbourhood frontman and Bad Guy singer dated for less than a year before a rep for the celebrity couple confirmed their split to PEOPLE back in May.

In the statement, they told the publication: “We can confirm Billie and Jesse did split amicably and remain good friends. All cheating rumors are false. Both are currently single.”

Billie Eilish reveals relationship status

After confirmation of Jesse and Billie’s split, fans wanted to know what the 21-year-old’s relationship status is now, as one asked: “Are you seeing anyone?”

She then responded by saying: “NO SIRRRRRR,” which seems like she’s happily single at the moment. The singer has had a busy summer touring around the US as she’s now heading off to Europe after performing at Lollapalooza Chicago.

Despite the busy schedule, fans are still waiting for her third album to drop, although Billie didn’t give away much when a fan asked about this one, dropping an elusive ‘zipped mouth’ emoji.

The star enters a ‘new era’ with hair transformation

Recently, Billie took to her Instagram to debut her red hair, which she showcased on stage at Lollapalooza. Many fans are saying it’s a ‘new era’ for the star.

As she leaned forward on stage, she debuted her dramatic hair reveal, as her roots had been dyed bright red, keeping her long black locks.

She then took the look over to Instagram which she captioned: “remember me?” leaving fans going crazy in the comments, as one wrote: “OMFG. just fell to my knees. NEW ERA??!!!!”
