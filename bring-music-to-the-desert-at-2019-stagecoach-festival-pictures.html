Luke Bryan, Cam, Lil Nas X + More Bring Country to the Desert at 2019 Stagecoach Festival [Pictures]

August 25, 2024
blog

Since 2007, Stagecoach Festival has taken over the Empire Polo Club in Indio, Calif., bringing an together an eclectic group of artists for a music filled weekend in the desert. The 2019 lineup covered the country spectrum, with Luke Bryan, Sam Hunt, Luke Combs, Cam, Kane Brown and more on the bill.

Several special moments were captured throughout the weekend, including an all-star collaboration between Lil Nas X, Billy Ray Cyrus and EDM DJ Diplo on Lil Nas X's "Old Town Road," Hunt and Combs sharing a laugh onstage and newcomers Jimmie Allen and Abby Anderson pairing up for a duet. Both Hunt and Cam took part in Diplo's afterparty culminating the end of the festival, with the "Burning House" singer and DJ both donning retro Western outfits.

Jason Aldean, Cole Swindell and Lauren Alaina also made an appearance at the 13th annual event. Scroll through to see all of the stars who ventured to California for 2019 Stagecoach Festival.
