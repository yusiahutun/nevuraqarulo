Hailey Bieber Elevated Her Winter Coat With Larger-Than-Life Shoulder Pads

February 03, 2024
blog

Hailey Bieber’s model-off-duty fashion has been at the top of its game lately. So far this season, she’s delivered numerous pantsless looks and has worn an infinite number of chocolate-covered ‘fits, and her latest appearance included one very good coat that is going straight to our winter mood board.

On Sunday, the Rhode beauty founder was spotted out and about in Tribeca singlehandedly bringing back the ‘80s strong shoulder trend in a burgundy peacoat that fell just above her ankles. The jacket featured larger-than-life shoulder pads and was layered over a black v-neck sweater, which she paired with a coordinating leather micro miniskirt and black sheer Calzedonia tights. Hailey accessorized with black patent leather heeled Mary Janes and a duffle-style maroon-colored bag.

She finished things off with tiny black sunglasses and dainty gold hoops, and she wore her brunette bob straight with a deep side part.

Hailey's winter wardrobe is filled with first-class outerwear. Before yesterday's outing, she was spotted in a full-length black Khaite coat with light-blue denim Dickie overalls. Ultra-feminine accessories — including black stilettos, futuristic shades, and a coordinating leather Saint Laurent bag — provided the finishing touches to her date-night look. Prior to that, she introduced the brown-on-brown-on-brown trend with a coffee-colored blazer and a brown leather micro miniskirt. Her go-to "B" diamond-encrusted necklace, chunky gold earrings, slingback kitten heels, and a deep red handbag slung over her shoulder rounded out her autumn-ready outfit.
