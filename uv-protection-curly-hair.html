January 31, 2024

ICYMI: The sun can take a toll on your hair, which is why sunscreen for hair is a thing. "UV rays can cause major damage to the hair cuticle," says natural hair expert Candace Witherspoon of the Candace Witherspoon Salon in New York City. "When the sun hits the hair for a long period of time, especially curly hair, it absorbs moisture and melanin. The curls react by becoming brittle and [the color can] fade." That's why it's vital to use haircare products that not only lock in moisture but also protect your strands from the sun. Ahead, we rounded up the best UV protection products for hair.
