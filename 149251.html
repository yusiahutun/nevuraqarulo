Samantha Sklar Promoted To Vice President Of A&amp;R At Position Music

May 04, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess)—Position Music President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Tyler Bacon and Partner and Head of A&R Mark Chipello have announced Samantha Sklar’s promotion to Vice President (VP) of A&R at Position Music.

Sklar’s most recent notable success is marked with the signing of breakout songwriter Jack LaFrantz, who co-wrote Benson Boone’s hit single “Beautiful Things.” The single recently hit No. 1 at both Top 40 and Hot AC, No. 3 on the Billboard Hot 100, is on its seventh week at No. 1 on the Billboard Global 200, and is currently 2024’s most streamed song on Spotify, with over 568M+ streams. LaFrantz co-wrote ten out of fourteen tracks on Boone’s debut LP Fireworks & Rollerblades, including singles “Slow It Down” and “Cry,” which currently sit on the Billboard Hot 100.

Sklar was listed as a “Favorite Publishing and Record Label Executive” by the artist trade group 100 Percenters in 2021. She joined Position Music in August 2022 and has been integral in helping to build out the frontline pop publishing roster. In her first few months, she signed producer/writer Dru “Falconry” DeCaro amid his success with platinum-certified “Numb Little Bug” by Em Beihold. The song reached No. 1 at Hot AC and entered the top 10 on Top 40 radio, peaking at No. 6. She has since brought in several other in-demand songwriters and producers, including Joe Kearns, Zach Skelton, John “Feldy” Feldmann, Alex “Abomb” Fernandez, among others to be announced.

“Sam Sklar is a dynamic and talented creative who is well deserving of this promotion and recognition,” says Chipello. She is a publisher through and through and has an incredible drive to serve writers and create opportunities for them. I am excited to watch Sam build this next chapter of her career, and I am proud to have her as a key part of our A&R team.”

” I’m excited to continue with this next chapter. Mark and Tyler have created an amazing culture at Position and have really empowered me to take the initiative to build as if this company were my own. We are in the thick of an exciting time of growth for Position as a whole. I could not be more thrilled with what we have been able to achieve thus far, and it’s truly only the beginning. I wake up every day grateful that I get to do this for a living, and fundamentally, none of it would be possible without all of the songwriters, producers and artists with whom I get to work. It’s that mentality that keeps me inspired and motivated to win for each and every one of them. As a result, taking this company to new heights,” says Sklar.
