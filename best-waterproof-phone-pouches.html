FAQ

Q: Are waterproof phone pouches worth it?

Waterproof phone pouches don’t necessarily protect your gadgets 100 percent, but they give them a much higher chance of survival. If anything, it could be an extra layer of protection for even further peace of mind.

Q: What is the highest waterproof rating?

IP68 is the highest waterproof rating you can get for waterproof phone pouches. An IP68 rating means that a device is fully protected against dust and can withstand being submerged in water up to 1.5 meters (4.9 feet) for 30 minutes. This level of protection is often found on devices like smartphones and smartwatches that are designed to be used in all environments, both wet and dry.

Q: Can you still use your phone in a phone pouch?

Yes, but keep in mind that it varies between cases. Some underwater phone pouches are noticeably better, while others make it quite difficult for your phone to respond to touch.

Q: Is there a waterproof phone case?

Yes, there are plenty of waterproof cases on the market, with Otterbox being one of the better brands. If you do pick a water pouch, having a waterproof case might be overkill, but at least your expensive device will be doubly safe.
