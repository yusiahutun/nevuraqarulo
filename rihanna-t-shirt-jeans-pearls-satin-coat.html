Rihanna Elevates Her T-Shirt and Jeans With Layers of Pearl Necklaces

May 16, 2024
blog

Only Rihanna could make jeans and a T-shirt look so grand and luxurious.

The “Love on the Brain” singer stepped out for dinner last night at her go-to spot, famed Italian restaurant Giorgio Baldi in Los Angeles. And while the base of her look included classic denim and a laid-back shirt, it was anything but casual. Rihanna cut the most stylish figure in low-waisted, light-wash baggy jeans with rips in the knees, paired with a boxy white graphic tee. She upped the ante white an opulent-white satin coat jacket that featured a large hood, three-quarter sleeves with wide cuffs, and a flared-out construction, along with dramatic pointed-toe heels from Gucci and a black Dior saddlebag.

The Hollywood Curtain/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

She didn’t stop there. For the pièce de résistance—or should we say pièces de résistance—the Fenty founder layered on an array of pearl necklaces of various lengths, sizes, and colors. She accessorized further with a diamond-encrusted silver watch, massive diamond statement rings, and narrow black shades. As for beauty, Rihanna glammed up with rosy blush and glossy muted-red lips, while styling her blonde curls in a pretty, sophisticated updo.

Rihanna has been serving up head-turning superstar looks this month. Last week, she channeled her inner rock star in a long shaggy cheetah-print coat from Bottega Veneta over another white graphic T-shirt, this time of Metallica from the band’s …And Justice for All era, along with baggy black snap-button jeans that she wore torn apart below the knee and matching knee-high combat boots. She finished this look off with black Gucci sunglasses, a chunky gold chain Gucci necklace, and diamond-adorned earring cuffs by Emily P. Wheeler.
