Tracking Kim K.'s Pregnancy Style

blog

Leave it to Kim Kardashian to shirk conventional maternity wear and opt for a rotating parade of super-sheer dresses, latex, and bodycon silhouettes. Ever since the reality star announced her second pregnancy, we (and the rest of the free world) have been keeping a close eye on her style and–although she’s barely showing–it’s clear her signature sexed-up style isn’t going away just because she’s carrying an heir to the West empire a baby boy.

When we spoke to Kim after her announcement, she hinted that comfort would be key throughout the pregnancy: “I’m definitely going to be more simple and I don’t care if I wear the same thing over and over, as long as it fits and I am comfortable. I’m a little nervous! Kanye didn’t help me last time. He helped me right after, he was like ‘Okay I can’t take this anymore, I’m gonna help you.’”

However, between that plunging latex pink Atsuko Kudo dress at a launch party in Nashville (pregnancy is no match for latex, folks), to an off-the-runway sheer Proenza Schouler look at the CFDA Awards, Kim’s proving that in her world, a baby bump is no reason to give up on bodycon.
