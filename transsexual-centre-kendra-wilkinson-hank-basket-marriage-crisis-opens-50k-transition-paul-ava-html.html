‘I always wished I was born a girl,’ London revealed. ‘Everything I did to my sister, I did to myself. I did her make-up, her hair, styled her. I wished I was my sister. But I never thought I’d transition.’

After she made the decision to become a woman, London underwent surgery in October of that year to get DD breast implants.

However she told Radar: ‘It was scary because I didn’t want to just get breasts and be a guy with breasts.’

London had been studying at the Fashion Institute Of Design And Merchandising in San Francisco, but dropped out.

Her boyfriend at the time paid for the $50,000 cost of further surgeries, and London was pleased she was able to have the procedures over the course of a short time.
