Selena Gomez and Nicola Peltzs best twinning fashion moments: the Hollywood BFFs have become inse

April 04, 2024

“We just clicked and had the best time,” Peltz recently shared in an interview with Cosmopolitan UK. “I feel like she’s my soul sister. I love her so much. She’s truly one of the most amazing people I’ve ever met in my whole life. The kindest person ever. Truly, her heart is gold. I feel like I’ve known her forever.”

Whether staying in or going out, the cute pair seem to like making a statement about their friendship by wearing complementary outfits. Let’s take a look at their best matching moments so far.

Silky pink pyjamas for a girls’ night in

Their first post as besties appeared last November on Peltz’s Instagram, showing the two cuddling on the sofa under the same blanket for a girls’ night. They both wore baby-pink satin pyjamas with fanciful ruffled trim at the straps and trouser leg from For Love & Lemons. They were celebrating the release of Gomez’s documentary My Mind & Me as Peltz wrote: “Girls’ night celebrating your beautiful documentary. Congratulations Selena! (Thanks b for the best food everrrrr).”

In other photos, Gomez’s nine-year-old sister Gracie Elliot Teefey and other friends are shown joining the pyjama party, enjoying food prepared by Peltz’s husband, Brooklyn Beckham.

Valentino sequin dresses for the New Year

Over a month later, the gal pals celebrated 2023’s New Year together in sparkly silver minidresses with sexy cut-outs at the waist and open backs from Italian luxury fashion house Valentino. They paired the dresses with translucent platform heels and hoop earrings. They even celebrated the occasion with special personalised embroidery adorning the labels inside their dresses, inscribed “Selena 2023” and “Nicola 2023”.

“Thank @maisonvalentino for my and my angel’s dresses! Felt like a fairy! Oh, and @isabelalysa for making us look good!” Gomez captioned the post.

Breezy white yachting outfits

After celebrating New Year’s, the duo embarked on a private yacht getaway with Brooklyn and some friends. Both girls chose white outfits to relax on the deck together. Gomez looks comfy in her cream-white baggy jumpsuit while Peltz opted for a cropped tank top and midi skirt that she paired with a white headband.

In another shot, Brooklyn also showed up in a white T-shirt to join them for a nap. “Fine calls us a throuple #foreverplusone,” Gomez joked.

During the same trip, the pair picked another matching white ensemble to soak up the sun, shown in another of Peltz’s snaps. The billionaire heiress continued to appear chic with a triangle-style bikini top and a headscarf, while the singer chose an elegant bandeau top. Both of them again teamed up with similar hoop earrings. “Love you so much,” the actress captioned the post, tagging Gomez.

Casual hang-outs in black

Both go for classic, sophisticated black for nights out. When they celebrated Peltz’s birthday early in a restaurant on January 8, the birthday girl dressed up in sleeveless black sheer gown with a flower tie on one shoulder while her pop star bestie wore a casual black jacket. The ladies also matched their buns and little hoop earrings while blowing each other a kiss.

A few days later, while celebrating the Only Murders In The Building star’s first Golden Globe nomination – for best performance by an actress in a TV series – Gomez opted for a strapless black dress while Peltz donned a black bodysuit with a black jacket, pairing it with jeans.

Colin Locascio furry hats over drinks

Most recently, in February, the two had a drink in cute faux fur hats from the New York-based Colin Locascio, known for its 1990s and Y2K youth culture inspiration.

Peltz went for a solid caramel-coloured hat while Gomez favoured green in the front of hers. They also matched in cosy black jumpers and, again, hoop earrings. “I love this girl so much @selenagomez angel sister,” Peltz wrote.

They even have matching arm tattoos reading “angel”! Peltz once shared a photo of them on her Instagram Stories.
