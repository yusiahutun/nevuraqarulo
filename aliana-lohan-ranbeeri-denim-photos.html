Aliana Lohan Is All Grown Up And Gorgeous In New Campaign For Ranbeeri Denim

September 30, 2024
blog

Lindsay Lohan's little sister isn't so little anymore!

Aliana "Ali" Lohan is the star of a new Ranbeeri Denim campaign -- and took to Instagram on Monday to show off a sneak peek of the stunning shoot.

"I am So Grateful and excited to be apart of this collaboration @ranbeeri_denim gets me and I get it. 'When you find something great stick with it and never let it go!' #denimisbeautiful," she captioned the black and white photo.

In the snap, the 21-year-old dons denim on denim in a jacket with leather sleeves and distressed jeans. She completes the look with a wide brimmed hat atop her fringed hairstyle and a guitar for a rock 'n roll vibe.

Do you see any shared of Lindsay?

Ali has kept a low profile lately -- but it could be because she's been busy working on her music career.

"I wouldn't exactly call it 'work' because I feel it's what I'm here to do," she tells Women's Wear Daily. "I'm creating new music and am very grateful to be fulfilling my dream. I'm so excited for the whole world to hear it and I feel many people will be able to relate."
