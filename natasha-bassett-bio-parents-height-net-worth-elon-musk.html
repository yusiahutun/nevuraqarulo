How Much Is Natasha Bassett Net Worth?

According to Wiki.ng, Natasha Bassett has a total net worth of around $5 million.

Natasha began her acting career at a very young age. At the age of 14, she went for the first audition that landed her a lead role in the Australian Theatre for Young People’s production of “Romeo and Juliet”. She also started writing and performing at the National Institute of Dramatic Art.

At the age of 19, Natasha was awarded a screenwriting scholarship from the ArtStart Screenwriters Program. There she made her directorial debut with her short film “Kite”. The film was featured in the Rhode Island International Film Festival, Balinale International Film Festival, and the Big Bear Lake International Film Festival.

Later, the actress moved to New York City and studied at the Atlantic Acting School, and later relocated to LA.

Some of her notable on-screen works to date are Camp (2013), The Last Goodbye (2013), Britney Ever After (2017), House by the Lake (2017), Spy Intervention (2020), Operation Buffalo (2020), The Pale Door (2020), and 12 Mighty Orphans (2021) among other movies and TV shows.
