Prior to marrying Tony, Becky was already married and separated from two husbands. Not only this but she has three children from her spouses before her marriage with Tony whom later he adopted. Let’s discuss in detail about Becky in the below sections.

Becky Robbins Before Meeting Tony Robbins

Born and raised in the United States, Becky Robbins holds an American nationality and belongs to the white ethnic background. She had a great interest in music and literature from an early age.

At the age of 18, Becky had her first marriage that produced three children. Nevertheless, the exact information about her former husband is out of the media’s reach. After some time, her ex-spouse left her along with her two sons and a daughter.

Struggling with the future, Becky didn’t understand what going on with her. Later, she decided to improve herself personally as well as professionally. She went to attend a motivational seminar by Tony Robbins titled Unleashing the Power Within.

Marital Life with Ex-Husband, Tony Robbins

While Tony Robbins was providing his speech, he noticed Becky Robbins in the front row of his seminar. Suddenly, the couple fell in love and started dating. During the time, Tony was 24 years old. After some time, the duo exchanged their wedding vows in 1984.

As soon as Tony knew about Becky’s three children, he adopted them all. After sharing nearly 14 years of married life together, the couple decided to part their ways. Both the couple filed for the divorce in 1998 which got finalized in 2001.

Since then, Tony and his current wife, Sage Robbins is spending a happy married life together. Currently, Tony is residing in Manalapan, Florida with his wife and children.
