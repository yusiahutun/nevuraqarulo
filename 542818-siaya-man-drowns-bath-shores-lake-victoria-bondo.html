Siaya - A cloud of grief and sorrow has engulfed Uhanya Beach in Bondo following the tragic death of a young man.

Identified as Samuel Otieno, the young lad is believed to have gone to take a bath on Tuesday, March 26, at the lakeshore when he fell into the waters and drowned.

How did Siaya man drown

Kimaiyo revealed officers from the patrol base visited the scene and conducted a preliminary probe into the incident.

According to the officers, the 26-year-old man was epileptic and had gone to take a bath at the lakeshore, where he developed an attack and fell into the water.

The police boss explained that local fishermen, through the help of members of the public, swiftly moved in to try to rescue him but only managed to retrieve him when already dead.

Kimaiyo added that the scene was processed and documented before the body was moved to the Bondo sub-county hospital mortuary.

Man drowns while bathing in stream

Earlier, a similar tragedy unfolded in Kisoma village as 34-year-old Paul Omondi Mbanda reportedly drowned in Ogege Stream.

Omondi reportedly drowned in a local stream where he had gone to have a bath in the afternoon.

Mzee Christopher Mbanda, Omondi's father, expressed his sorrow following the sudden death of his son, who had left home in good condition.

Mbanda said they found his son's lifeless body floating downstream after his supposed drowning.

According to the local police officers from Ukwala, Omondi might have lacked control over his body after suffering an epileptic seizure.

Mbanda's body was taken to Ukwala Hospital mortuary.
