Everlane The Ultra-Fine Merino Ribbed Cardigan

I’m a sucker for a matching set, so naturally, this merino ribbed cardigan and the coordinating shell tank are already in my shopping cart. The 100 percent wool sweater, which is currently 30 percent off, is “super soft and lightweight” with a “flattering” cut, according to one reviewer. Shoppers also praised the design’s “comfortable” fabric, while one person noted that it has “ample sleeve length, which is hard to find for tall girls.”  

Everlane The Cashmere Oversized Turtleneck 

While cashmere is definitely a luxury purchase, think of it more as an investment — and one that will last you a lot longer than fast fashion styles. This 30 percent-off oversized turtleneck, in particular, is a staple sweater that “won’t date,” according to one shopper. It also has a “timeless” silhouette, according to one customer who raved about the quality that continues “to hold up well after multiple seasons.” The material is crafted from 94 percent recycled cashmere and both sourced and spun in Prato, Italy. 

Everlane The Long Re:Down Puffer 

Whether you’re ready to admit it or not, winter is on the horizon — and that means it’s time to purchase a puffer if you haven’t already. Everlane’s knee-length, relaxed fit features a center-front two-way zipper with double pulls and a hood that boasts internal drawstrings for extra protection. The coat itself is filled with recycled down feathers and lined with a recycled polyester to ensure warmth. The 40 percent-off style comes in olive green and black, so it’s versatile to wear with practically anything. 

Everlane The Alpaca Wrap Sweater

This alpaca wrap sweater is one of my favorite styles on the Everlane site because it offers practical warmth and a perfectly polished aesthetic, and it’s 30 percent off. The material is made from responsibly-sourced alpaca fiber, recycled polyester, and merino wool that one shopper described as “lofty, light, and warm without being overly warm.” The silhouette is slightly cropped with ribbed detailing and a tie-waist that defines your frame.
