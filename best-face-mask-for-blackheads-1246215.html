Those pesky and grainy bumps on your face may seem harmless but they can cause more damage to your skin than their stature. Many of us have experienced these bumps, famously known as the blackheads, especially on our noses and T-zones, at least once in a lifetime. To combat it, we end up using facial scrubs, which can be harsh on the skin. But drawing from our experience, we can let you in on a better secret —- the best face masks for blackheads. These are relatively more gentle than traditional scrubs. 

Blackheads occur when pores get blocked due to excess oil and dirt accumulation. This buildup gets oxidized due to exposure to air. By using one of the best masks for blackheads, you can gently break down residue and dissolve it to give smoother and clearer skin. With continuous use, the masks may also prevent reappearance. Besides clearing blackheads, some masks also include acne-fighting ingredients like benzoyl peroxide, kaolin clay, etc., which makes them perfect to be added to any acne skincare routine.

    How to Select the Best Face Masks for Blackheads?

    Here are a few factors to consider for choosing the best blackhead face masks. 

    Ingredients 

    When it comes to ingredients, it's ideal to look for formulas made with natural and organic ingredients. Exfoliating active ingredients such as salicylic acid ( a beta hydroxy acid) dissolve dead cells and remove excess oil. Glycolic acid (an alpha hydroxy acid derived from sugarcane) also breaks debris and cleanses skin. On the other hand, absorbent ingredients like kaolin clay and bentonite clay absorb sebum to minimize pore clogging and shrink large pores.

    Skin type 

    It's important to know your skin type while shopping for masks for blackheads. This will help you pick the right option depending on your skin type concerns. For example, people with dry skin may choose moisturizing and hydrating masks that remove impurities and blackheads without making the skin tight and flaky. Hypoallergenic and mild formulas are perfect for sensitive skin types. Those with oily skin may opt for non-comedogenic masks that dissolve extra oil, leading to blackheads due to residue accumulation.

    Fragrances 

    People sensitive to fragrances must avoid scented options. They may opt for unscented or mildly scented options that don't trigger allergic reactions. 

    Application 

    The best facial masks for blackheads are available in different forms like peels, clay, sheet masks, etc. Since these masks absorb oil, you have to be careful about the duration of the application so they don't strip off natural skin oil. Typically, they are applied for 15 to 20 minutes. However, you can check the duration given on the instruction label to be more precise. 

    Now that you have understood how you can choose the best face mask for blackheads, here's the right way to use it. 

    How to Apply a Facemask to Remove Blackheads?

    Follow the given steps for using the blackhead facemask correctly. 

  • Cleanse your skin using a mild cleanser. Dry your face using a towel. 
  • Pick your favorite mask, maybe clay, peel-off, or sheet mask. 
  • Apply the mask to your face. 
  • Prevent using it on eyes and lips. 
  • Let the mask stay for 15 to 20 minutes or as per the instruction label. 
  • Remove the mask and wash your skin with a mild cleanser and normal water to remove leftovers.
