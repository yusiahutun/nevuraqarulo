Early Life

Kyle Richards was born on January 11, 1969, in Hollywood, Los Angeles, California, the United States of America, to father Kenneth Edwin Richards and mother Kathleen Mary Richards.

Both her father and mother are dead. Her dad passed away in 1998 while her mom died in 2002. The couple had split in 1972 after which Kathleen married two more times.

She has two siblings Kathy Hilton (her half-sisters from her mother's first marriage to Lawrence Avanzino) and Kim Richards.

She also has three paternal half-siblings from the first marriage of her father. All of whom were adults when she was born. She has two half-nieces Nicky Hilton and Paris Hilton.

Married Life With Husbands Guraish Aldjufrie & Mauricio Umansky

Kyle Richards is a twice-married woman. She has currently been married to a real estate agent Mauricio Umansky.

The couple first met in 1994 and after two years of dating, they tied the knot on January 20, 1996, when the actress was 4 months pregnant. The baby was born on June 18, 1996; it was a girl who they named Alexia.

They have two more children, daughters, Sophia (born in 2000) and Portia (born in 2008). The family currently resides in Bel Air, Los Angeles.

Kyle was previously married to her ex-husband Guraish Aldjufrie. She was 18 years old and was pregnant with their daughter Farrah when they exchanged vows in 1988. The former couple called it quits in 1990 and got a divorce two years later.
