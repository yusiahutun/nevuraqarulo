This history of the Chanel 19 Bag: 

The bag Meghan wore is distinctive due to it’s oversize stitching and it’s provenance. In fact the Chanel 19 bag's origins can be traced back to (you guessed it 2019) and the last collection of the revered Karl Lagerfeld who designed it together with his protégé and eventual successor Virginie Viard. The name “pays tribute to its elder, the 2.55 bag, created in February 1955 by Mademoiselle Chanel,” the brand explains of the style on its website.The bag became an instant hit after its debut, thanks to its contemporary twist on the classic flap style which was reimagined it for the 21st century audience. Just like its predecessor the bag is also named after the year of its creation.
