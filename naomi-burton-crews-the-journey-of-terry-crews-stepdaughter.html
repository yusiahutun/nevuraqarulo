Naomi Burton Crews: The Journey of Terry Crews' Stepdaughter

While many individuals shine under the glitz and glamour of fame in Hollywood, others carve their path in the shadows, making their presence known through their unique journey. Naomi Burton Crews is one such individual. Although a TV personality, singer, and actress, she is famous as the stepdaughter of renowned actor Terry Crews. 

Her stepfather, Terry Crews, is a celebrated actor, television host, and former football player. His dynamic career includes his portrayal of Julius Rock in the UPN/CW hit sitcom “Everybody Hates Chris,” which aired from 2005-2009. Due to her sheltered lifestyle, many are curious about who she is.

Here’s a look into the life and journey of Naomi Burton Crews, from her origins to her contributions to the world of entertainment and beyond.

Who Is Naomi Burton Crews?

Born in the United States in 1989, Naomi grew up in the serene setting of Greenwood Village, Colorado. She was born into the family of Rebecca King-Crews and her ex-husband, Charles Burton. Her father, Charles Burton, remains a relatively enigmatic figure, with little information available about him. However, Naomi’s mother, Rebecca King Crews, is an actress, singer, and fitness instructor known for her versatile talents. 

According to reports, Naomi’s mom, Rebecca, was in Junior college at 21 when she married Charles Burton. Since it was an early marriage, the marriage crumbled quickly, and the former couple went their separate ways. However, the reason behind their divorce is unknown, and Naomi Burton Crews is the sole child of the union.

Since she wasn’t born into fame, Naomi kept her childhood and education relatively private. Despite her sheltered upbringing, she pursued her academic endeavors with determination. Consequently, she holds a bachelor’s degree in Psychology and Sociology from the University of Denver. 

In addition, she further honed her expertise with a master’s degree in Clinical Psychology from the University of Southern California, showcasing her commitment to education and personal growth.

When Did Naomi Burton Crews’ Mom Marry Terry Crews?

The chapters of Naomi’s life intertwined with fame when Rebecca King Crews crossed paths with Terry Crews. After her divorce from her first husband, Naomi’s mom met Terry in Western Michigan while serving as the music director at her local church. According to reports, Terry walked into service and observed the choir director, and not long after; he decided to make her his wife.

The couple tied the knot on July 29, 1990, and their encounter in Western Michigan birthed a love story that spanned three decades. After their wedding, Terry decided to become the father of Rebecca’s child, Naomi, who was about a year old. Hence, the famous Hollywood star legally adopted her, changing her name to Naomi Burton Crews. The details of this transformation remain private, yet it signifies a profound bond that extends beyond blood ties.

Due to the adoption, Naomi has had a comfortable life, gaining popularity, especially after starring in the Black Entertainment Television (BET) reality show “Family Crews” alongside other members of the Crews family.

What Does Naomi Burton Crews Do?

Although Naomi Burton Crews has introduced herself as a singer, writer, and actress, her accomplishments span various fields, showcasing her multidimensional talents. In addition to being an entertainer, she pursued a career as a clinical psychologist and licensed marriage and family therapist.

Her educational journey saw her obtain a bachelor’s degree in Psychology and Sociology from the University of Denver. After completing college, she got her Master’s degree in Clinical Psychology from the University of Southern California, where she majored in Marriage, Family Therapy, Child Welfare, and Trauma.

However, Naomi’s life beyond her achievements remains a well-guarded secret. As a single mother raising her daughter, Miley Crews, she navigates life with grace and strength. While maintaining a private social media account, she embraces opportunities that align with her passions, contributing to her estimated net worth of $1 million.

How Many Siblings Does Naomi Burton Crews Have?

As part of the Crews family, Naomi Burton Crews has four younger siblings. First is Azriel, born in 1990 and has followed in her father’s footsteps in acting. Next is Tera, who was born in 1999. Although she works in advertising, she has an acting bug. She has also featured in “Family Brainsurge” and “Terry Saves Christmas,” in addition to “The Family Crews.”

After Tera comes Wynfrey; she was born in 2004 and has yet to forge her career path. However, she likes photography and often posts beautiful photos on Instagram. Last but not least is Isaiah. He was born in 2005 and is the youngest of the Rebecca King-Crews children. In addition, he has the most acting experience. 

The teenage TV star appeared in the 2020 Nickelodeon series “Side Hustle.” He is also a rising social media sensation; he posts amusing TikTok videos dedicated to his burgeoning fandom. Also, he enjoys football and likes watching it with his father.
