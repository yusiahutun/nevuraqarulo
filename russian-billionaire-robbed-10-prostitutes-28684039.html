A Russian billionaire has been robbed of around £250k by a gang of 10 prostitutes after getting him paralytically drunk.

The unnamed oligarch, who is a 45-year-old resident of the Rostov-on-Don area, has been described in local Russian media as going under the name of Igor.

He travelled to Moscow earlier this year, alongside his wife and daughter, for business.
