The Modern Icon: Ganni's Buckle Ballerina flats

July 17, 2024
blog

It's common knowledge that Ganni does great shoes. From its Western-style cowboy boots, and go-with-everything loafers to the sell-out trainer collaborations with New Balance, the Scandi favourite knows exactly what we want from our footwear today. Now, there's a new style working its way into the most stylish wardrobes: the Buckle Ballerinas.

Fashion fans will have spotted the shoe all over their Instagram feed, on the streets of fashion week, and at Ganni's spring/summer 2024 catwalk show led by model of the moment, Paloma Elsesser. Featuring a comfortable block heel, a pointed toe, slingback straps and statement silver buckles, we can see why the shoe has become so popular.

Christian Vierig//Getty Images

The style continues Ganni's best-performing shoe, with the brand reworking it in a host of colourways to meet the high demand. The slightly punkish take on the ballerina comes in a classic black, statement-making red, elegant cream and a fun snakeskin that's perfect for breaking up neutral layers. And, as we enter a new season – and the popularity of the shoe shows no sign of slowing down – Ganni has released four new colours to invest in: a bright blue, the autumn/winter 'It' shade brown, as well as an olive green and beige.

"It’s a style that we first launched in 2021 and it has become a favourite of our community since, but as with most good things, it was a slow burn," notes Ganni's creative director Ditte Reffstrup.

What does the mastermind behind the Scandi-cool brand think makes a modern icon? "The Buckle Ballerina embodies timeless style along with a contemporary edge," she notes. "The modern twist of the buckle detail adds a hint of rebellion, making it a statement piece in its own right."

The story behind the shoe

"The Buckle Ballerina draws its inspiration from the intersection of classic sophistication and a more contemporary everyday look," says Reffstrup. "We wanted to create a shoe that captures the essence of the modern woman, someone who appreciates comfort without sacrificing style. It's a nod to traditional ballerina flats, but with a fresh twist to make it uniquely Ganni."

Ganni

As Reffstrup says, it's surprisingly tricky to find pieces today that feel both distinctive and versatile: we've all got at least one statement piece at the back of our wardrobes that looks beautiful, but doesn't actually go with much. "It's a shoe that effortlessly complements different styles, from casual to formal, making it a great fit for many occasions. I think people are drawn to pieces that can seamlessly blend into their wardrobes, and our ballerina does just that."

Who has worn them?

At the Danish brand's spring/summer 2024 catwalk show in Copenhagen back in August, countless fashion editors and influencers were wearing the Buckle Ballerina. It has fast become a firm favourite amongst the fashion crowd, so it's no wonder the style keeps selling out.

Christian Vierig//Getty Images

That's the beauty of these practical flats – they can be styled with anything, worn wherever and for the whole day, thanks to their comfort. Team with floaty dresses in the warmer months, then wear with cosy socks come winter. "I love to see them styled with a mini dress or a tailored suit," says Reffstrup. "It creates a balance of femininity and strength, which is a reflection of the Ganni aesthetic."

"What excites me most is witnessing the creativity of our customers in how they style the shoe. It's incredibly rewarding to see it adapt to their unique tastes and expressions of style."

Where to shop

Shop the Buckle Ballerina from Ganni's online store, MyTheresa, Net-A-Porter, Farfetch and more of our favourite retailers. Can we expect any new releases soon? "We're always exploring new colourways and textures," says Reffstrup. "Right now, my favourite colourway is white: it adds such a great balance to darker-toned looks as we enter autumn." As if we needed any convincing to add another pair to our wish-list.
