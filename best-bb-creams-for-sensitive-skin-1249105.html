    What to Look for In a BB Cream for Sensitive Skin?

    1. Assess Your Skin Type

    One of the most important factors to consider before buying the best BB cream for sensitive skin is whether you have oily or dry skin type. If your skin is oily, you should go for non-comedogenic products that won’t lead to breakouts. However, if your skin is dry, you should consider using BB creams with nourishing formulations. You can look out for ingredients like jasmine, and plant-based oils like jojoba, sunflower, etc. to seal in the moisture.

    2. Check the Shade

    To complement your natural skin tone, it’s important to buy a BB cream that enhances your skin texture. You should go for a shade that neither feels too dark nor too light for your skin. Sharing a pro tip, our contributor Dr. Zeeshan Afzal, Health Content Advisor - Welzo, an online healthcare platform, says, “For sensitive skin, it's best to choose a BB cream shade that matches your natural skin tone. Opt for a hypoallergenic and fragrance-free product to minimize the risk of irritation.”

    Do a swatch test and get a shade that offers medium coverage to get a flawless complexion.

    How Should You Apply BB Cream on Sensitive Skin?

    Step 1: Cleanse your face thoroughly.

    Step 2: Apply a pea-sized amount of moisturizer. You can also use toner/serum if you include them in your skincare routine. If you have oily skin, you can skip using moisturizer if your BB cream has a nourishing formula.

    Step 3: Take a dollop of BB cream and apply it evenly on your face. You can use your fingers, brush, or sponge to blend it on your face.

    Step 4: You can follow it up with the foundation if you need more coverage.

    Step 5: Use sunscreen to complete the routine. If your BB cream has decent SPF protection, you can skip this step.

    FAQ's

    Can you use BB creams on sensitive skin?

    Yes, you can use BB creams on sensitive skin.

    Is using BB cream every day safe for your skin?

    Yes, BB creams are safe for your skin but ensure you follow a proper skincare routine to keep your skin oil-free and clean.

    How is BB cream different from CC cream?

    BB cream offers medium coverage, unlike CC cream which is designed to offer full coverage. BB cream may also feature more benefits for sensitive skin types.

    Should you apply moisturizer before using BB cream?

    Yes, you should apply moisturizer before using BB cream if you have dry skin to smoothen dry patches.

    Can you set a BB cream with powder?

    Yes, you can set a BB cream with powder if it doesn’t absorb properly.

    Can you sleep with BB cream on your face?

    No, you should avoid sleeping with BB cream on your face.

    How long does BB cream last on the face?

    BB cream may last on the face for 6-7 hours. However, it may vary depending on the formulation of the cream.

    Are there BB creams with SPF protection for sensitive skin?

    Yes, BB creams with SPF protection for sensitive skin.

    How should you remove BB cream from sensitive skin?

    You can use micellar water and makeup wipes to remove BB cream from sensitive skin.
