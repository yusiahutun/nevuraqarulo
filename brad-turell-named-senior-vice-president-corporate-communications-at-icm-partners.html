Brad Turell Named Senior Vice-President, Corporate Communications At ICM Partners

April 06, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Veteran agent Brad Turell has joined ICM Partners as Senior Vice-President, Corporate Communications. Turell will work with Chris Silbermann, Managing Partner of ICM Partners to lead global strategic communications and branding strategies for the agency.

Turell comes to ICM Partners from Turell Media Strategies, a consultancy he launched last August after leaving Paradigm following a nine-year tenure at the agency.

Prior to Paradigm, Turell spent eleven years at Time Warner serving as Executive Vice-President, Network Communications at The WB and head of Worldwide Corporate Communications at Turner Broadcasting Systems. He began his network executive career at Fox, where he was Senior Vice President, Publicity and Talent Relations.

Turell will be taking over for ICM's longtime head of corporate communications Michelle Suess, who announced that she was leaving the agency last week.
