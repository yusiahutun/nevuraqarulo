Hailey Bieber Just Launched Her First-Ever Eyewear Collection

September 15, 2024
blog

Aside from having impeccable fashion sense (both on and off red carpets), Hailey Bieber is known for stomping the runway, interviewing celebrities in her bathroom, and sharing bits of her life with more than 42.2 million followers on Instagram. Though it's clear the multi-hyphenate certainly has her hands full, she just added another line to her already impressive résumé: co-designer for a new collaboration with Vogue Eyewear.

The launch of the new line, which was announced on March 31, includes four sun styles and four optical frames to celebrate the "beginning of a new season." The cohesive collection comprises of trendy earthy tones and metal frames in unique shapes akin to styles the supermodel often wears herself.

Never one to keep the same style for long, Hailey stressed the importance of versatility when creating her eyewear. "Vogue Eyewear is known for having unique styles for everyone, and I believe eyewear is an accessory that can elevate your look," Hailey shared in a press release. "With this collaboration, we focused on designing options that could fit with any style."

The collaboration's first drop aims to combine "authentic beauty with celebrity style" while embodying "every facet of [Hailey's] personality." The eight frames are now available for purchase and the supermodel said the collaboration's creative process is what she treasured most from curating the line.

"This collaboration is special to me because I was able to work with the team to co-design frames that felt natural, but still fashion-forward and fun," she said. "The Vogue Eyewear team was so much fun to work with and we had a great time shooting the campaign for the collection."

The Hailey Bieber x Vogue Eyewear collection is available now, and you can purchase each pair for $99 exclusively at www.Vogue-Eyewear.com.
