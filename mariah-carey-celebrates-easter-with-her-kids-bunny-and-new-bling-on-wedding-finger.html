Singer sets internet ablaze showcasing a ring on her wedding finger in adorable Easter video

    Mariah Carey has set the internet alight after posting an adorable family video over the Easter break holding a baby bunny … and wearing a set of diamonds on her ring finger.

    WATCH: Mariah celebrates by jumping off a boat in a hot pink wetsuit

    Clutching the fluffy grey rabbit and surrounded by Monroe and Moroccan - her 11-year-old twins from her marriage to ex-husband, Nick Cannon - the singer blew a kiss into the camera and said: "Happy Easter, we love you."

    Eagle-eyed fans were quick to spot the row of diamonds on her ring finger, with some questioning whether her boyfriend of six years, Bryan Tanaka, has popped the question.

    In another shot, slim Mariah, clad in a tight black top, leggings, signature heels, sunglasses, diamond earrings and bracelets, crouches down towards the baby bunny and says, "Look at his little ears. Does he eat, like, little carrots and stuff?"

    The singer was married to music mogul Tommy Mottola from 1993 to 1998 and tied the knot with TV host Nick, 42, before splitting in early 2014. Their divorce was finalised in 2016.

    When romantic Mariah – who refers to birthdays as anniversaries – turned 54 last month, she received a special tribute from Bryan. The choreographer and creative director, who was a backup dancer on Mariah's 2006 'Adventures of Mimi' tour,  posted a black-and-white photo of the couple to his Instagram on her birthday, 27 March. The caption read: "This is one of my favorite pictures. Ever. Happy Anniversary my Beautiful Queen."

    View post on Instagram 

    The couple began dating in 2016 after the singer ended her engagement to Australian billionaire James Packer and she went public with Bryan in February 2017, posting a photo of the lovebirds kissing on her socials. They enjoyed a whirlwind romance on tour, spending holidays together and traveling the world. Although they briefly split after just five months of dating, they’ve been going strong ever since, posting numerous shots of them embracing.

    Meanwhile, star Nick welcomed his 12th child in November. Zeppelin Cannon is his third child with Abby De La Rosa - they also have 20-month-old twins Zion and Zillion.

    Nick has three children with Brittany Bell – sons Golden, six, seven-month-old Messiah Rise, and daughter Powerful Queen, three. He also co-parents four-month-old daughter Legendary Love with model Bre Tiesi and seven-month-old daughter Onyx with former Price Is Right model LaNisha Cole.

    He also shares Halo daughter Halo Marie Cannon with Alyssa Scott. Tragically, their five-month-old son Zen died in December 2021 due to brain cancer.
