Elvis Presley's manager sold "I Hate Elvis" badges.

During the recording of "I Want to Hold Your Hand," The Beatles incorporated handclaps to enhance the song's rhythm and add a distinctive element to their music. This innovative approach contributed to the track's infectious energy and timeless appeal.

The iconic band ABBA chose their name by combining the first letters of their first names.
