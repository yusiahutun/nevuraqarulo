Reverend Lucy Natasha is one of the most prominent pastors in Kenya who commands a huge audience.

Natasha is a senior pastor at Empowerment Christian Church Nairobi and has been dubbed 'Kenya's hottest preacher'.

The woman of God has been Kenyan men sleepless nights with her curvaceous body. But beyond her beauty, she delivers powerful summons locally and internationally.

According to her LinkedIn profile, she studied theology at the South African College of Applied Psychology.

Lucy Natasha's early life

The beautiful pastor was raised alongside two siblings by her mother Esther Wanjiru who's also a preacher and her father, a businessman.

Her brother Shafique heads the protocol team in her church while her sister Shiphra is a pastry chef.

Natasha was raised in Mathare slums in Nairobi and was a stammerer according to Who Owns Kenya. She learnt to speak well when she attained the age of nine and that's how her journey in the ministry began.

Lucy Natasha's career

The city preacher officially started spreading the gospel when she was 19 years old. She administered the word of God in villages and towns.

On June 19, 2011, she was ordained as a reverend by Arch-Bishop Gitonga of the Redeemed Church.

Natasha is the founder of the Prophetic Latter Glory Ministries (PLGM).

Lucy Natasha's wealth

The flamboyant woman is married to Prophet Stanley Carmel and the two live a lavish life as shown by beautiful cars, vacations, huge homes, and nice clothes.

Rev Natasha disclosed she gets flowing cash from her partners, International Ministry Missions and royalties of her books.

"We have partners that watch our services, those that attend Miracle Mondays and those that watch from around the world. I am also an author of more than 12 books, I am an entrepreneur so there are multiple streams of income,” she said.

Private jet

As reported by Pulse Live, the cleric acquired a brand new private jet in 2019.

The Oracle of God preacher uses the jet to fly around the world with her team spreading the gospel.

“I don’t know why people make it a big deal when a preacher gets a private jet and they don’t do the same when a politician does the same. The Kingdom of God needs money and speed if we are to reach out fully to this generation… I trust that if Jesus was preaching during these days, he would be using such facilities,” she said after acquiring the jet.

Luxurious vehicles

Natasha drives around in luxurious state-of-the-art vehicles.

Some of the vehicles she owns are Toyota Harrier, worth approximately KSh 4 million according to Nairobi News and a Toyota Fortuner, worth an estimated KSh 3.5 million.

Rev Natasha also owns a sleek Mercedez Benz E class that costs about KSh 4.5 million besides other cars.

Lucy Natasha's books

Apart from preaching the gospel, Rev Natasha is an accomplished author who has over 10 books to her name.

Some of her publications include Seven Keys to Success, Touching Heaven through Prayer, Every Problem Has Expiry Date, Characteristics of God’s Leading Lady and Before You Say ‘I Do’.

Oracle Group of Companies

The elegant preacher is the executive director of Oracle Group of Companies.

She has investments in real estate.
