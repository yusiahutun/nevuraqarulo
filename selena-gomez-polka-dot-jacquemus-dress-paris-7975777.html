Selena Gomez's Polka-Dot Minidress Had the Most Dramatic Sleeves

July 19, 2024
blog

Paris Fashion Week is supposed to be about the looks coming down the runways, but if Selena Gomez and her singular style have anything to do with it, all eyes are on her outfits as she's taking in the sights and sounds of the City of Light. Today, just hours after giving us all a masterclass in LBD dressing, the superstar did a full fashion 180, swapping her black dress for a white one with the most over-the-top shoulders we've seen in a while.

While making a stop at Paris's L’Avenue restaurant today, Gomez wore a super-short polka-dot minidress from Jacquemus. While the dress was pretty straightforward, with its abbreviated hem and square neckline, it had two huge, puffy statement-making sleeves. Gomez finished the look with a pair of soon-to-be-released geometric silver hoop earrings from Mejuri, pointy slingbacks from Versace with the brand's signature Medusa logo, and a sweet and sleek black bag from Jacquemus. She kept her makeup glowy and simple and had her hair parted down the middle.

While it's tough to replicate Gomez's fashion sense (it comes with a little help from her stylist, Erin Walsh), it's easier to get in on her makeup game. People reports that Gomez and her BFF Nicola Peltz Beckham recently wore identical makeup looks, which they showed off on Instagram. In a Story post, Gomez and Beckham both had glowing complexions, dramatic blush, and glossy pink lips. The post comes just a day after the two were seen together at a soccer game in the French capital.
