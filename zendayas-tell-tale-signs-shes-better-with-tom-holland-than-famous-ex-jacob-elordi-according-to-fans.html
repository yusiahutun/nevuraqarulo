Zendaya fans have identified two tell-tale signs they believe she’s happier with her boyfriend Tom Holland than with her ex, Jacob Elordi.

Tom Holland and Zendaya have been one of Hollywood‘s most adorable couples for a while now. The pair met while working on the Marvel blockbuster Spider-Man: Homecoming in 2016, giving way to romance rumors before their relationship was confirmed in 2021. It was recently reported that they have spoken about the possibility of marriage, and how perfect they are together cannot be denied. Fans are even comparing how she acts around him in stark comparison to her ex…

Fans’ tell-tale signs Zendaya is better with Tom Holland than Jacob Elordi

Zendaya was reportedly romantically linked with co-star Jacob Elordi after the pair began starring in the hit HBO TV series Euphoria. Rumors surrounding the pair began to take flight in the summer of 2019 after they were spotted out and about on several occasions, although nothing was explicitly confirmed by the pair.

However, a fan recently shared a compilation of images to TikTok, presenting Zendaya and Jacob together in comparison to how she acts around Tom in footage and photos.

After watching the clip, fans have jumped in to argue two tell-tale signs that Zendaya’s better with Tom than Jacob. “She radiates happiness and comfort with Tom,” a fan commented, receiving an overwhelming 62.9k likes in agreement.

“I would too, he seems so genuine and kind,” one replied.

Observing Zendaya’s clear happiness in contrast to the stills showing her with her ex, a commenter weighed in, “A woman in love just radiates.”

“It’s called: when you find your right person,” another suggested of the celebrity couple.

Ultimately, the tell-tale signs of happiness and comfort are illustrated beautifully by her body language around her partner. “The way she’s constantly leaning into him,” a fan beamed.

Zendaya and Jacob Elordi romance gossip didn’t last long

By the time of December 2019, talk of a full-fledged relationship blossoming between Zendaya and Jacob simmered down after the Australian actor described her as being “like my sister” during a GQ interview.

Despite such a remark dismissing speculation they’d become anything more in the future, he was sure to offer her high praise.

“She’s super dope to work with,’ he exclaimed. “She’s an incredible artist and a very caring person to all of us. But we’re all really close. There is not one weak link in that show. We’ve spent so much time together and everyone is just so cool to work with.”

Although, like Jacob, she met Tom through work, it perhaps couldn’t be clearer that she’s found the one.
