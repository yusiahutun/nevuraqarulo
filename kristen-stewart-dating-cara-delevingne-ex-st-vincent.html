Is Kristen Stewart Dating Cara Delevingne's Ex, St. Vincent?

August 04, 2024
blog

AKM-GSI

Kristen Stewart is reportedly dating music artist St. Vincent (née Annie Clark), Cara Delevingne's ex-girlfriend, according to Us Weekly.

Rumors of their romance began when Stewart, 26, brought Clark, 34, as her date to the New York Film Festival screening of her new film Certain Women. "They spent almost every day together after that," a source told the magazine. They were soon spotted together again the following day in NYC (above).

News of the new couple follows shortly after both the singer and the actress' recent breakups.

Clark's split from Delevingne was announcedin September, after they were together for a year and a half. Their long distance relationship became "too much," causing them to break up, according to The Sun.

Getty Images

Stewart, on the other hand, was recently dating actress Alicia Cargile. "I'm just really in love with my girlfriend," she said of Cargile in the September issue of Elle UK. "We've broken up a couple of times and gotten back together, and this time I was like, 'Finally, I can feel again.'" The last time the two were spotted together was on Aug. 27, according to Us.
