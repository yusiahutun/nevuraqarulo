Holly Willoughby launches new Dunelm homeware range and prices start from just 10

blog

Holly Willoughby fans can now get their hands on the TV presenter's new Dunelm homeware collection.

The collection includes new categories such as curtains, bedspreads and pillowcases that come in pretty floral patterns lending to a fresh, tranquil style - and the best news is, prices start from as little as £10.

With the winter months soon behind us and as we look forward to the spring season - now is the perfect time to out with the darker, cosier furnishings in our homes and replace them with lighter and brighter homeware items.

The new Catalina bedding collection has been crafted with 100% cotton percale with the blue trailing spring florals providing a gentle refresh.

The Carmella bedding collection which includes a warming pink elegant flowers and foliage to finish off the soft supple pure cotton percale.

Holly commented on the collection, saying: "We have all lived in our homes so much over the last 18 months and to have some new things to bring into our home just to refresh it all a little bit is just a lovely thing to do.

"When I'm designing a range like this, something I like to think about is the fact that people have already invested in pieces, so I like to have new things that work with some of the the old things, so it feels updated.

"We have things for a long time and we like pieces that are more sustainable that we can keep and cherish, so that's always in the back of my mind when I am designing something now.

"The new collection was inspired by wanting to bring the outside in, I think that nature has become important to all of us, I think we have all felt the benefits of all of it, so bringing a little bit of the outside into your home just feels like a lovely thing to do.

"What really makes a house a home is the people within it, home needs to be somewhere where you can feel safe, where you're not too scared to put your feet up on the sofa, you want to surround yourself with things that feel nice as well that have a certain quality to them."
