The Top 6 Events To Attend In The Stuart Area This March

April 14, 2024
blog


Here’s what you should mark down on your calendar. 

1. Resort at Home Interior Design Studio

Make your house the dream home you have always imagined with design tips from the pros at this event on March 1. Mark your calendar for the next session already scheduled for April 5. (Free; facebook.com/resortathome1)

2. Pizza Party

Discover how pizza and prosecco can be the perfect pair. On March 13, head to Tack & Jibe Nautical Eatery in Stuart for a Prosecco, Cava and Pizza Party from 5:30 p.m. to 9 p.m. Enjoy bubbly drinks like Tiamo prosecco and Cava, a sparkling wine from Spain. Plus, guests who stay for dinner will receive 15 percent off their bills. (tackandjibeeatery.com)

3. Majestic Marine Life

Show your love for the Treasure Coast’s gentle sea cows during Manatee Appreciation Day on March 29. Fort Pierce’s Manatee Observation & Education Center will observe the national holiday celebrating these marine mammals, which takes place the last Wednesday in March. (manateeeducationcenter.org)

4. A Coupl’a White Chicks Sitting Around Talking

Transport yourself to suburban Westchester County, New York, where two women form an unlikely friendship and team up against their husbands. This long-running Off-Broadway comedy will be in town March 11 to 26. ($25; actstudiotheatre.com)

5. Make Helping A Habit

Support Habitat for Humanity of Martin County at a charity auction with private dinner parties, sports lessons, portrait sessions and a day in the life of a fireman or sheriff all up for grabs. The Mr. Stud Finder Charity Auction at the Frances Langford Dockside Pavilion in Jensen Beach will take place on March 9 at 6 p.m. and will feature a cocktail reception with hors d’oeuvres. ($50; habitatmartin.org)

6. Shamrock Fest 

Break out your most festive green garb for this St. Paddy’s Day party filled with live music, bagpipers, whiskey, beer, Irish fare and more. The March 17 party will run from 4 p.m. to 10 p.m. at Gazebo Park. ($6/in advance, $10/at the door; kaleidominds.com)
