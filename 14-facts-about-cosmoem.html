Cosmoem is an Ultra Beast Evolution

Cosmoem is a unique Pokémon that evolves from Cosmog, and it is the pre-evolution of the powerful Legendary Pokémon, Solgaleo and Lunala. It is classified as an Ultra Beast due to its mysterious nature and cosmic powers.

FAQs

1. How do I evolve Cosmoem into Solgaleo or Lunala?

To evolve Cosmoem, you need to level it up to level 53. Once it reaches this level, it will evolve into Solgaleo if it is in the Pokémon Sun version, or Lunala if it is in the Pokémon Moon version.

2. Can I catch Cosmoem in the wild?

No, Cosmoem cannot be caught in the wild. It can only be obtained by evolving a Cosmog, which itself is a rare Pokémon that can be received as a gift or in special events.

3. What are the abilities of Cosmoem?

Cosmoem has the ability “Sturdy,” which prevents it from being knocked out by any attack that would normally faint it in one hit. This ability can be very beneficial in battles.

4. Is Cosmoem a Legendary Pokémon?

Yes, Cosmoem is considered a Legendary Pokémon. It plays a crucial role in the games’ storyline and has unique characteristics that set it apart from regular Pokémon.

5. Can Cosmoem be bred?

No, Cosmoem cannot be bred as it is classified as a “Nebula Pokémon,” which cannot produce offspring. It can only be obtained through evolution.
