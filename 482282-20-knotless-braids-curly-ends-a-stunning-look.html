Quick facts about knotless braids with curly ends

  • How long do knotless braids with curly ends last? If done right and taken care of well, knotless braids can last up to three months. Other factors like texture and hair porosity also greatly determine how long you can stay with your braids.
  • How do you curl the ends of knotless braids? The easiest way to curl the ends of your knotless braids is to dip them in hot water. Use rollers to roll your braids, and cover your face with a towel to avoid getting burned. You can also utilize a curling iron to curl the ends of your knotless braids.
  • What hair is good for knotless braids? Boxer braids produce the best curls. They produce tighter curls with more complex curl patterns.
  • Is knotless better than box braids? Knotless braids are always a better alternative because it creates less tension. Therefore, you have a lesser chance of traction alopecia and follicle damage.
  • Why are knotless braids so expensive? Since knotless braids involve adding hair down the length of the braid, it takes more time than regular braids hence costly to install.
  • Can you wash your hair with knotless braids with curly ends? Yes, you can wash, but apply lathering shampoo and conditioner through the hair with a downward motion to keep the braids smooth.
  • What should I put on my hair before knotless braids? It is a good idea to moisturize your hair before installing braids. The better hydrated it is, the healthier your hair will be during the entire time you have braids.
  • Should curly hair be braided wet or dry? Braids are ordinarily installed on dry hair. Therefore your hair should be dry during the installation.
