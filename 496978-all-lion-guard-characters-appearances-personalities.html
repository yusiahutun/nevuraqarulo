Lion Guard characters

The Lion Guard animated TV series storyline is simple. It is a team of animals whose duty is to protect the Pride Lands and the Circle of Life by all means. Their leader is Kion, and they often meet at the Lair of the Lion Guard.

The fact that the animated TV series is based on one of the biggest animated films from Disney, The Lion King, makes it even more enjoyable. And the characters are even more fascinating, from their naming sequence and what they represent. So, who are the characters, and who is the strongest lion in the Lion Guard?

1. Fuli

Fuli is one of the most outspoken characters on Lion Guard. Fuli is a cheetah who acts as the group’s strategist and one of the vices of reason because of their immense wisdom. Kuli comes up with some of the most brilliant ideas during times of need. Another exciting thing about Fuli is that they act as the group’s ambassadors, convincing other animals to join the guard. Fuli’s voiced by Diamond White.

2. Bunga

Bunga is one of the main characters of the Lion Guard and is Timon and Pumba’s adoptive nephew and Kion’s best friend.

Bunga’s battle cry is “Zuka Zama!” which he often mentions. In season 3, Bunga meets another honey badger named Binga, and they fall in love. Bunga’s boldness sets him apart from other animals in the Lion Guard.

3. Kion

Kion is a lion, Simba’s and Nala’s second-born cub, and Mufasa’s grandson. He is also Kiara’s younger brother, the Prince of the Pride Lands, and the leader of the Lion Guard. According to the show’s narrative, Kion is the fiercest.

Kion always tries to do the right thing, and the show’s story revolves around him. He and the Night Pride’s leader Rani, fall in love and become rulers of the tree of life in the series finale. Max Charles voices Kion, and the singing voice is of Aaron Daniel Jacob in Season 3 except for Battle of the Pride Lands.

4. Ono

Ono is the bird in Lion Guard and is quite popular. The bird serves as the Lion Guard’s strategist and is also a voice of reason in the group. Ono’s incredible intelligence allowed him to create the best solutions to emergent problems.

Ono’s voiced by Atticus Shaffer and is one of Kion’s best friends. Unfortunately, even though Ono had the keenest eyes of the Lion Guard, they were damaged while saving Bunga in Battle for the Pride Lands.

Ono relinquishes his role to Anga, but Kion still preserves him as part of the Lion Guard by giving him the role of the most intelligent member.

5. Beshte

Beshte (which means friend in Swahili slang) is a hippopotamus and serves as a leader of the Lion Guard. Dusan Brown voices the character. Beshte is the strongest Lion Guard member and Kion’s friend. The hippo often exclaims “Poa” (okay in Swahili slang), and his battle cry is “Twende Kiboko!”

6. Anga

Anga is an eagle and is a proud member of the Lion Guard. Bryana Salaz voices the character. Anga takes over Ono’s role as the Keenest of Sight. The character has a fantastic ability to speak to other animals, bringing out complex issues simply. Anga is also the keeper of the sun.

7. Jasiri

Jasiri is a spotted hyena, one of the most popular Lion Guard characters, and a great supporter of Kion and the Lion Guard. Jasiri lives in the Outlands and is the leader of the clan.

Jarisi learns of Scar’s spiritual return to the Outlands in the second season and forms the Hyena Resistance. After the defeat of Scar during season 3, Jasisir becomes the leader of the Outlands.

8. Makucha

Makucha is a leopard in the Lion Guard and is in charge of his army and leap. In season 3, Makucha plays the primary villain. The leopard comes out as fiercely protective and independent.

Makucha is voiced by Steve Blum. Blum's incredible sound reveals the cunning and devious character who loves hunting rare animals.

9. Ushari

Ushari is a cobra in Lion Guard. The snake was initially based in the Pride Lands but relocated to the Outlands in season 2. Ushari is one of the villains in the animated TV series and is short-tempered. Before leaving the Pride Lands, Ushari would get disturbed whenever the Lion Guard was near him. In season 3, during the Battle for the Pride Lands, Ushari is knocked over by Bunga and falls into the Outlands’ volcano.

10. Makuu

Makuu is the crocodiles’ supreme commander. Being a crocodile himself, he depicts the typical character of a crocodile in the real world. Brian Underwood voices Makuu. However, in season 2, the crocodile reforms from his troublemaking ways.

Makini, voiced by Landry Bender, is a young mandrill that becomes Rafiki's apprentice. Interestingly, Makini is the only character aware that Kion speaks to the spirit of Mufasa. Later, in season 3, Makini is made Rani’s Royal Mjuzi.

12. Tiifu

Tiifu, which loosely translates to obedient in Swahili, is one of the most popular characters in Lion Guard. The character is voiced by Sarah Hyland and plays a supporting role. Tiifu is one of Kiara’s closest friends, along with Zuri.

13. Reirei

Reirei is a female jackal that resides in the Outlands in the Lion Guard TV series. The female jackal is the matriarch of a family of jackals and is one of the most manipulative villains. Reirei is voiced by Ana Gaesteyer and is one of three antagonists in the Lion Guard, with Shupavu and Mzingo. She was a member of Scar’s army. Reirei has a habit of kidnapping members of the Lion Guard to use as leverage.

14. Nuka

Nuka is one interesting character. For a lion, you wouldn’t expect such a character to be part of the Outlands. Nuka appeared in the episode Lions of the Outlands. Nuka has a strange appearance of brown fur with grey fur on his mouth and underbelly.

Nuka’s character isn’t good. He is hot-headed and often jumps into fights without measuring up to his opponent well. On top of that, he is cowardly at times.

15. Kovu

Kovu is the leader of the Lion Guard and is an adult male lion. The lion is Zira’s youngest son and the younger brother of Nuka and Vitani. Even though Kovu is often sober and level-headed, he can sometimes be hot-headed and impulsive. One great thing about him is that he will always do whatever it takes to protect his family and friends.

Who is Kion's daughter?

Shani is the second daughter of Kion and Rani. She is tomboyish, energetic, playful, easy-going, carefree, reckless, daring, and mischievous. She is the little sister of Raja and Sawa and the adopted sister of Baraka. Her elder sister is Sawa.

Who married Kion?

Kion marries Rani after accepting her proposal. After marrying, they are confident that they will make great leaders. They go ahead to have several children, including Shani.
