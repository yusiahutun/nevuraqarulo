B.J Thomas' biography

Thomas was born on 7th August 1942 in Hugo, Oklahoma, in the United States. His parents are Vernon Thomas (father) and Geneva Thomas (mother). Later, his family relocated to Houston, Texas, where he was raised.

His father was an alcoholic and an extreme lover of music, especially country songs. He has two siblings, a sister Judy Thomas and a brother Jerry Thomas. After his elementary studies, he joined Lamar Consolidated High School in Rosenberg.

He broadened his musical horizons by listening to various genres such as soul, gospel, and rock while still a young boy. Some of his favourite artists were Mahalia Jackson, Jackie Wilson, and Little Richards. He also sang in the choir as a teenager.
