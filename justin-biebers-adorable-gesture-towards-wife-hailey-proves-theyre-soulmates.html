Justin Bieber's adorable gesture towards wife Hailey proves they're 'soulmates'

May 12, 2024
blog

Justin Bieber paid a heartfelt tribute to his wife, Hailey Bieber on her 27th birthday, sharing an adorable PDA video.

The celebrity couple have been married since 2018, and it seems like their marriage is going from strength to strength, with Justin’s adorable birthday post.

Justin Bieber pens sweet birthday tribute to Hailey Bieber

Taking to his 292 million Instagram followers, the Yummy singer shared a black-and-white video of a cute PDA moment, as he plants kisses on his wife’s forehead and lips.

In the caption, he penned: “Happy birthday baby. You make life wonderful. And I’m the lucky one that gets all of you [love heart eye emoji]”

As well as her cute post from her hubby, Hailey also received balloons and a scrumptious cake from the Rhode team, as well as birthday wishes from celebrity friends including Kim Kardashian.

The model was certainly feeling the love, as she took to her Instagram story to write: “Thank you for all of the birthday wishes. 27 is feeling so cozy, love.”

Later on, she posted a video of herself and her music star husband releasing Chinese lanterns into the sky, an act that is thought to bring happiness and health. 

Fans took to the comments on the post to gush over the happy couple, as one penned: “IDC what ANYONE says they’re the definition of SOULMATES.”

One joked: “Why wasn’t it me god.”

“You two deserve nothing but all the love and happiness! Happy birthday Hails,” commented another.

The couple first met in 2009

Although they didn’t start dating until 2014, the pair actually met all the way back in 2009.

Something to show their grandkids, their first meeting was even caught on tape, at The Today Show. Hailey’s father, Hollywood star Stephen Baldwin introduced the two, who shared a brief handshake.

Little did they know that nine years later, they’d be tying the knot.
