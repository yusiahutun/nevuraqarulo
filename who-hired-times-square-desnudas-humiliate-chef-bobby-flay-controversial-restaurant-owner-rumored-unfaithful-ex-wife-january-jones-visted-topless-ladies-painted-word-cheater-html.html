Time Square's topless desnudas have been hired by a mystery client to ambush celebrity chef Bobby Flay.

The semi-naked ladies suprised the restrauntuer at the New York City Wine and Food Festival where they paraded round with the word 'cheater' painted across their bare chests.

Flay recently divorced his third wife, Law & Order actress Stephanie March, amid rumors he had several affairs - including an alleged relationship with Med Men star January Jones.

But the 'desnudas' - a Spanish word meaning naked - are not giving away the game as to who hired them for the stunt to humilite Flay.

Flay (left) recently divorced his third wife, Law & Order actress Stephanie March (right), amid rumors that he had several affairs during their decade-long marriage

Their manager, Chris Oliveri, 25, who believes the Time Square street performers could use their new-found fame for a reality-TV show or U.S. tour, said: 'I was paid a lot of money not to say anything.'

However March's lawyer denied her client was involved and said it could have been 'any of the disaffected people in [Flay's] world.'

It's not the first time someone has gone out of their way to embarass the chef.

'She says they had sex many times and in different places, including the London hotel in Los Angeles.'

In June 2010, TMZ claimed that Bobby showed up to the scene after 37-year-old January crashed her car.

At the time, the 50-year-old said they had watched a basketball game together at the London hotel the previous evening and that the actress has asked for his number because she wanted his advice on redoing her kitchen.

He added to the website that they had only met once before that evening, and he didn't know why January had called him after her accident.

Last month, a source told the New York Daily News that Stephanie, 40, had questioned Bobby about whether he was romantically involved with January, following his appearance at the car crash scene.

'I know Stephanie basically asked him after January Jones, 'Did you f*** her?'' an insider told the site, adding that Bobby denied anything untoward was going on.

In Touch's source claims: 'They had a huge fight over January shortly before Stephanie asked him to move out of their place. There is communication between Bobby and January that indicates they were more than acquaintances.'

The magazine goes on to allege that Law And Order: SVU star Stephanie claims in the divorce documents that Bobby also cheated on her with his assistant and a food stylist. 

January, whose son Xander was born in September 2011, is currently rumoured to be dating her The Last Man On Earth co-star Will Forte.

A report also appeared in New York Post's Page Six which claimed that the break up was down to him having an affair with his 28-year-old assistant Elyse Tirrel.

Flay has vehemently denied the affair.

His team accused March of digging dirt in order to get a larger divorce payout which was limited by a prenup between the couple.

'We will continue to refrain from responding to the continued efforts by certain parties to spread rumors and innuendo,' his rep said in response.

'This specific allegation was in a letter sent from one attorney in this case to the other. It was written and then leaked specifically to try to insert this story into the press, and that's unfortunate.'
