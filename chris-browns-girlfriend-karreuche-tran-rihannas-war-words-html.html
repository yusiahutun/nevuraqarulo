There is no love lost between Rihanna and Chris Brown's new girlfriend, Vietnamese model Karrueche Tran, according to new reports.

The two women have become embroiled in a very public feud, according to Radar Online and The New York Post.

The tension is said to have kicked off when Rihanna posted a photograph of a bag of rice cakes wearing sunglasses with gold earrings, and the caption, 'Ima a make u my b****.'

War of words: Rihanna is said to be embroiled in a very public feud with Chris Brown's new girlfriend Karrueche

This has been widely perceived as a jibe at Tran, who of Vietnamese origin.

Further proof is provided on Rihanna's remixed song Birthday Cake - which saw her sensationally reunited with Brown.

On the sexually explicit track, she sings the line: 'Remember how you did it?, Remember how you fit it?, If you still wanna kiss it, Come, come, come and get it, Sweeter than a rice cake, cake worth sipping.'

She then responded to a follower who wrote: ‘@rihanna save me some rice cakes rather hungry,' with the rather acerbic comment: 'Kinda DRY tho.'

Dig? Rihanna posted a picture of a bag of rice cakes, gold earrings and sunglasses, which could be perceived as a dig at Karrueche

Retort: Rihanna retorted that the rice cakes are 'kinda dry tho' on her Twitter

In response, Karrueche is reported to have posted on her Facebook page: 'I’m Angeline (sic), you’re Jen. C’mon you see where Brad is at.'

However, this was later shown to have been a comment made by one of Rihanna's followers.

She then is alleged to have added: 'Let me clear the air, if you have fenty at the end of ya name or your default Is Rihanna you have a better chance of winning the lottery than ending up on my friends list.'

However, Karrueche has denied that she even has a Facebook page.

Yesterday Rihanna posted another missive on Twitter which said: 'I’m a leader, yall on some followin s***... coming in dis game on some modelling s***', quoting lyrics from Lil Kim/Mob Deep’s Quiet Storm remix.

Ride or die: Brown and Karrueche seen on holiday together recently and she has posted tweets of their domestic life including a soul food feast

Her comments could have been construed as a thinly veiled dig at Karrueche, who is a model.

Denial: Chris Brown has denied he is back together with Rihanna

According to the New York Daily News, Rihanna and 22-year-old Brown are rumoured to  have been 'seeing each other in secret.

'Rihanna loves to live dangerously, and talking to and hooking up with Chris is all part of that,' a friend told Us magazine of the couple’s potential rendezvous.

However, the couple have both dismissed the persistent rumours that they are back together.

Rihanna recently told Jonathan Ross on his TV show that she missed being in a relationship.

'Single life is so overrated,' she said.

'It sucks. I have such incredible experiences in my life. You don’t want to live your life and then meet someone.

'You want to share your life with someone. That’s what I’m missing right now.”

Meanwhile, a representative for Brown has denied the R&B singer is back together with Rihanna.

'He and Rihanna are just friends,' the representative told Us. 'He’s not cheating.'
