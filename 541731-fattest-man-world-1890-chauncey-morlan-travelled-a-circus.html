Chauncey's father, James Nelson Morlan, was a businessman. He engaged in mercantile business at Rockville until he died in 1901. James Nelson Morlan was married twice. His first marriage was to Miss Margaret Kirkpatrick, by whom he had one daughter, Ella Morlan Graham.

After Margaret, he married Martha Ann Royse Morlan, by whom he had two sons, Chauncey Morlan and James Morlan. Before marrying James Nelson Morlan, Martha Ann was in a previous marriage and had welcomed a son named Charles.

The personality grew up in Indiana and attended a local school in Rockville, Indiana. His struggle with weight started when he was a child. By the age of seven, he weighed 140 pounds.
