Harold Ray Tracy Sr. | Obituaries

March 05, 2024
blog

Harold Ray Tracy Sr., 81, of Laurel passed away Tuesday, March 12, 2024 at his residence in Laurel. He was born Monday, Nov. 2, 1942, in Natchez.

A memorial service will take place at a later date. Ellisville Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements.

He was preceded in death by his parents Ralph E. Tracy Sr. and Laura Brown Tracy; sisters Ruth Lipscomb, Mary Allen and Dorothy Bradshaw; and brother Ralph E. Tracy Jr.

Survivors include his wife Sherrill Frances Tracy; daughters Sindy Tracy Connell, Cindy Stevison and Sharon Conner; sons Harold Tracy Jr. and Grady Tracy; brother to dad Tommy Allen Tracy; 13 grandchildren; and 12 great-grandchildren.
