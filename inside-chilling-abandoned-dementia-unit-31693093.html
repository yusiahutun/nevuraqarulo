Inside chilling abandoned dementia unit that looks like it was left in a hurry

August 28, 2024
blog

An urban explorer has taken a chilling look around a former dementia home still fitted out with pictures of its former residents.

What makes this former care centre so chilling is that it looks modern, a ghostly reflection of the kinds of facilities so many Brits have visited loved ones in. That is because this Audenshaw, Manchester home only closed its doors this year, according to the man who took a look around, Exploring With Jake.

The veteran urban explorer, who has previously spoken with the Daily Star, went into the all-too-familiar building, its medical and clinical appearance still desperately masquerading as a cosy living room.

Things are made even more bleak by Jake, real name Jake Parr, who revealed images of signs up around the home that have the names and faces of former residents outside their doors. The furniture is hospital-style – all straight lines and no frills. Is it a teacher’s lounge or an accident and emergency ward? Without context, it would be hard to tell.

It is believed the home is called Fairfield View which, according to the NHS, had 54 beds and has been regulated by the Care Quality Commission. The commission deemed Fairfield as “requires improvement”.

It noted the facility needed improvement in terms of safety, effectiveness and responsiveness. It was deemed “inadequate” in terms of whether or not it was well led although it was deemed “good” in terms of its care.

The home was operated by Fairfield View Care Limited which, according to Companies House, has overdue accounts and confirmation statements at the time of writing. A further look reveals the company went into administration in November 2022. All seven officers listed on the database are marked as “resigned”.

The images Jake took are chilling. Armchairs with their attached tables all lined up together like they're watching a TV that isn't on. Personal effects, like a toothbrush and a bracelet, were found to remain in the facility, it's like everyone just vanished.
