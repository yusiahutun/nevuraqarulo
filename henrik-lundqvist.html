Biography TimeLine

Henrik Lundqvist is a former Swedish professional ice hockey goaltender widely regarded as one of the best goalies of his generation. He was born on March 2, 1982, in Are, Sweden. Lundqvist started playing hockey early and began his professional career in 2000. He spent most of his career playing for the New York Rangers in the National Hockey League (NHL), where he became the team’s all-time leader in wins and shutouts. Lundqvist has also represented Sweden in numerous international tournaments and helped his country win the gold medal at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin. In September 2021, Lundqvist announced his retirement from professional hockey due to a heart condition.
