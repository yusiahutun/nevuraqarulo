Love Island 2021 contestants to wear BadHero sunglasses range from ISAWITFIRST

June 07, 2024
blog

Love Island 2021 finally kicked off last night (Monday) and fans were happy to welcome the singletons back to our screens after 18 months.

As the official fashion partner for this year's Love Island, ISAWITFIRST will be supplying all of the Islanders with their statement outfits throughout the show.

The retailer will also be the official sunglasses partner for the hit ITV dating show, and has launched the BadHero range of shades for the sexy singles to 'crack on' in style.

The Love Island Drop is the first collection available to the public, which comprises 114 designs that were curated by the show’s stylist in collaboration with other members of the production team.

For the entire length of the show, all frames in the BadHero collection will be available for £75 down from £129 - how good is that?

Get a further 50% off the range using code: QUICK50

Natalie Jordan, Buyer at ISAWFIRST said: “We’re excited to be back as a Love Island partner this year, with a very special element that we’ve never done before – new BadHero sunglasses, that will be supplied to the islanders during their stay. There will be regular drops throughout the series and we hope the range will get people talking - as much as all the drama in the 2021 show.”

The Love Island collection features full spectrum polarisation – a technology that cuts out dazzle and glare in all directions - perfect for a cheeky chat in the sun.

As well as boasting polarised lenses, there is also a full range of lenses and tints available in a mood-boosting colour palette, contrasted with trendy acetate frames in a wide spectrum of shapes.

All BadHero sunglasses boast UV400 protection, providing nearly 100% protection from harmful ultraviolet light rays, including UVA and UVB rays.

But that's not all, The Core and NightFade collection will launch on July 2, giving customers even more ways to change their look.

Designed to be worn at night, the NightFades feature a range of eight styles with transparent frames and five tinted lenses including pink, blue, orange, lilac and yellow.

Customers keen to save some extra pounds on their next purchase can do so with TopCashback.

All you have to do is be a new member and sign up for a free account. You'll then find the ISAWITFIRST offer and spend a minimum of £10 to be eligible for the £10 cashback offer.

Aware of its responsibility to create a more sustainable fashion landscape, BadHero are offering those who get tired of their sunglasses the chance to send their old pair back in a pre-paid envelope and receive 50% off a brand-new style of their choice.

BadHero will then donate the old pair to charities who provide eyewear to those in need across developing countries.

Full credit is also available against a new pair of sunglasses should the original model be lost, damaged or stolen, while the brand will also replace scratched lenses free of charge within 12 months of purchase.
