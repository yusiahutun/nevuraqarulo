Meghan Markle wears shorts for polo match in Santa Barbara

July 31, 2024
blog

The Duchess of Sussex is here to convince you to invest in a pair of shorts this summer. The royal – who attended a polo match that husband Harry was playing in on Sunday – looked impossibly chic in a pair of white, tailored shorts, which she styled with a polka-dot blouse and a wide-brimmed hat.

The royal made a rather glamorous addition to the sporting event, which took place at the Santa Barbara Polo & Racquet Club, where she helped pass out prizes to Harry and his winning team.

Shutterstock

Embracing the beautiful sunny weather, Meghan opted for a pair of high-waisted, loose-fitting, tailored shorts by New York label Khaite, a brand she has worn on a number of occasions, including recently during the Invictus Games, where she teamed one of the label's bodysuits with a pair of black trousers.

The duchess added a chunky black Khaite belt to her polo-match look – and paired them with her trusty black Aquazzura court shoes and a long-sleeved polka-dot blouse. She finished the look off with a pair of statement sunglasses and a wide-brimmed black straw hat.

This is not Meghan's first shorts outing of the season, either. The duchess wore another pair of white shorts to one of Harry's polo matches earlier this month.

The slightly shorter style was teamed with an oversized white shirt and a pair of flat sandals, offering a more relaxed and casual look for the duchess.

If you don't have a pair of shorts in your wardrobe, then we suggest you let Meghan inspire you to change that.

Where many of us tend to favour dresses and skirts rather than shorts during the summer months, a great pair can actually help to transform your wardrobe, whether that's for low-key occasions, something more dressed-up or even for a day in the office.

In fact, a great pair can actually take you through the seasons too. Choose a high-waisted, knee-length style and you'll be able to team them with knee-high boots and a blazer when the temperatures drop, while they are also easy to style with flat or heeled sandals during the summer.
