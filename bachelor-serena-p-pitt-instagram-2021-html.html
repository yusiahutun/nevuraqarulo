When Chris Harrison introduced The Bachelor Season 25 cast, the host promised Serena Pitt — aka Serena P. — will have an immediate connection with Matt James. That said, the contestant hasn’t had much screen time. And of course, Bachelor Nation fans are turning to social media to find out more about the 22-year-old publicist from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. So what is Serena P.’s Instagram? The cast member’s feed highlights her sense of humor.

Serena Pitt is from ‘The Bachelor’ Season 25 with Matt James

While James was 28-years-old while filming The Bachelor Season 25, it certainly seems Serena P. was not afraid of the 6-year age difference. According to her Bachelor Nation profile on ABC’s website, Serena P. has always believed that “no matter what your age is, when you know, you know.” Then when the couple met on night one, it was clear that age wasn’t even a factor.

In The Bachelor Season 25 premiere, Serena P. walked out of the limo with a step stool. The contestant revealed she stands 5 feet 2 inches tall, whereas James is 6 feet 10 inches. 

“My name’s Serena and I heard that you’re very tall,” Serena P. told James with a smile. “I’m only 5’2” so I brought a little something to help me get on your level.”

Then as the night went on, Bachelor Nation viewers could see a spark between Serena P. and James as they played a game of chess together. It also looks like the couple will get some one-on-one time in The Bachelor Season 25 Episode 3.

“Serena P. finds out if her initial feelings for Matt could develop into something more serious,” the ABC press release for the Jan. 18 episode reads.

Where Bachelor Nation fans can find Serena Pitt on Instagram

While Bachelor Nation fans learn more about Serena P. on the ABC reality series, viewers can find James’ cast member on Instagram under the handle @serena_pitt. 

The Canadian contestant frequently posts photos from her everyday life and travels. Within the past few years, she’s traveled to places like Jamaica and Budapest. She also just graduated from Wilfrid Laurier University in 2019.

Meanwhile, Serena P.’s Instagram page is sprinkled with a bit of humor. Not only is every caption a fun one-liner, but the first line of Serena P.’s bio states that she’s not related to actor Brad Pitt. The Bachelor Season 25 contestant also made some jokes about her time on the show. That said, Serena P. hasn’t hinted how she’s doing now that filming has wrapped. 

Chris Harrison says Serena Pitt will be honest with Matt James on ‘The Bachelor’ 2021

Whether Bachelor Nation fans turn to Serena P.’s Instagram feed or simply go by her time on the ABC reality series, it’s clear she’s one to watch. But, this won’t come as much of a surprise to fans who watched Harrison introduce the cast back in December 2020.

“She may be small, but Serena P. is a force to be reckoned with on the show,” Harrison said at the time. “Great chemistry with Matt. It is evident from the moment she steps out of the limo. They just have this chemistry. They have this thing.”

The host also teased what Bachelor Nation viewers can look forward to next.

“The thing to keep an eye on — this is very interesting — she’s always honest,” Harrison said of Serena P. “She always tells everybody, including Matt, where she stands, how she feels, what the score is. Now, will Matt hear that? We’ll have to find out.”

The Bachelor Season 25 with Matt James airs Monday nights on ABC.
