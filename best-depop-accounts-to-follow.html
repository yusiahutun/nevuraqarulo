The 10 best Depop accounts to follow for hidden fashion gems

February 17, 2024
blog

Since the pandemic, many of us have shifted our shopping habits towards a solely online existence. Gone are the days of unflattering changing room lighting and exhausting return queues, we're all about scoring some stylish bargains at the mere swipe of a finger.

Plus, nowadays plenty of us are more conscious of our wardrobe's environmental impact and so, second hand clothing marketplaces present a solid alternative to fuelling the whirlwind that is fast fashion.

While Depop is renowned for its Y2K-obsessed user base, the 2000s-inspired trend certainly isn't the only aesthetic on offer. Granted, the app does boast swathes of retro corsets, halter scarf tops and a mountain of cargo pants, but also, it's an excellent option if you're looking to bag a vintage Prada nylon number, some Dior shades or a retro Penny Lane coat.

Hello! Fashion picks our must-follow Depop accounts for hidden fashion gems:

Best for...funky prints

Studio Pascale Eliza ought to be your first port of call when it comes to cool prints - think Vivienne Westwood baroques, Jean Paul Gaultier pop art pieces and the like.

Best for...one-of-a-kind samples

Fans of Alexia Elkaim's cult-adored label Miaou will be thrilled to hear that they can now get their hands on one-off archive pieces from the fashion set-adored designer on Depop at Miaou Archive. Look out for daring leather cuts and waist-cinching corsets.

Best for...vintage footwear gems

For pre-owned designer shoes, check out Awww 90s. The shop features plenty of utility designs and logo-emblazoned pieces, but when it comes to Prada footwear, this account is a total treasure trove.

Best for…satin pieces

Christie's Cupboard is a treasure trove for all things 1990s – it feels very 'young A-lister on the red carpet' with a hint of prairie. Expect exquisite coloured leather jackets and ultra-flattering slip dresses.

Best for…leather outerwear 

Tanis Vintage is perfect if you're on the hunt for a sleek pre-loved leather jacket. From blazers to motocross styles, go hell for leather with this extensive crop of outerwear.

Best for…noughties It-girl

Isabella Vrana's Depop shop is giving Buffy the Vampire meets Lizzie McGuire – and frankly, we're very much here for it.

Best for…hidden gems

Antique diamonds, one-of-a-kind signet rings and stunning craftsmanship – if you're in the market for some vintage jewels, check out The Silver Store. Immediately.

Best for…cool cut-outs

Alice Zielasko really ought to be on your radar if you're into playful Rixo prints, quality accessories and bags of leopard print. Which, obviously, you are.

Best for…Doc Martens

For the Dr. Martens-obsessed among us, RoseTintedDocs is a brilliant shout, more than capable of satisfying all your utilitarian footwear needs.

Best for…the cool-girl aesthetic

Specialising in 1990s and 2000s vintage, Susan Sus basically sells the supermodel dream. The picks feel very Bella Hadid, and the selection includes hand-crafted knitwear as well as plenty of low-rise styles.
