Considered the popping and tutting queen, Dytto is a notable American dancer. 

She rose to fame after uploading her dance video in a song titled Barbie Girl Trap Remix in 2014. Soon after, she got massive attention and responses from fans, which became her stepping stone to success. 

After her video got viral, Dytto made an appearance in Ellen: The Ellen DeGeneres Show in September 2015. Later, Dytto made her theatrical debut in 2018 via a comedy movie titled Life of the Party.

Apart from her appearances on TV and the big screen, Dytto has also hosted a music show titled The Drop.
