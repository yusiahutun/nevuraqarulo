The identity of Will Arnett's new girlfriend has been revealed.

The funnyman, 43, who was married to Parks and Recreation star Amy Poehler, is now dating producer Erin David, according to US Weekly.

Arnett brought David to Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux's star-studded holiday party last weekend.

Caught by surprise: Will Arnett took his new girlfriend to Jennifer Aniston's holiday party

Arnett and his new lady love met on the set of the sitcom Up All Night, on which he starred opposite Christina Applegate and Maya Rudolph. Erin was an executive producer on the series, which ran for two seasons, from 2011 to 2012.

Arnett and Poehler, 42, married in 2003 and had two sons together: Archie, five, and Abel, three. Their divorce became final in 2012. Arnett was previously married to actress Penelope Ann Miller but that union lasted just over a month.

After divorcing Poehler, Arnett had a brief relationship with celebrity chef Katie Lee, 32, who was married to singer Billy Joel for six years.

Famous exes: Will was once married to fellow Saturday Night Live alum Amy Poehler

'Will and Katie Lee fizzled. He lives in LA, she lives in NYC,' a source told US Weekly. '[Erin] produced one of the shows or a film he was in. She produced a lot of Lorne Michaels stuff.'

Aniston, 44, and fiance Theroux, 42, have an annual tree lighting party at their Bel Air home. The guest list included: Courteney Cox and her new beau, Snow Patrol rocker Johnny McDaid, Robert Downey Jr. and his wife Susan Levin, Tobey Maguire and his wife Jen Meyer, Jason Bateman, Isla Fisher, Chelsea Handler, and Gwyneth Paltrow and Chris Martin.

Arnett's new gal pal once worked with Poehler on the 2008 film Baby Mama.

Poehler has also moved on. She is dating comedian and actor Nick Kroll, 35.
