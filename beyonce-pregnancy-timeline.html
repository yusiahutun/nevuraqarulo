Beyonce's Pregnancy Facts and Dates

July 04, 2024
blog

When Beyoncé announced that she and Jay Z were expecting twins, the result set an Instagram record. Not that it's at all shocking, since she is the queen, after all. Blue Ivy is set to be a big sister, and her mom posted every moment from her pregnancy in the form of picture-perfect social media posts. And yet, she still managed to keep the details intensely private.

But she hasn't posted any news since the end of May, which makes fans think something is up. As speculation swirls about whether Bey has welcomed her twins, take a look back at a pregnancy that has had everyone watching.

February 1, 2017

Beyoncé announces her pregnancy on Instagram. “We would like to share our love and happiness,” she wrote in a caption. “We have been blessed two times over. We are incredibly grateful that our family will be growing by two, and we thank you for your well wishes.” Artist Awol Erizku confirmed he was behind the artistic photoshoot.

Seven hours later

The announcement becomes the most-liked Instagram photo of all time, besting Selena Gomez.

February 12, 2017

Beyoncé performs at the Grammy Awards, singing a medley of songs from her album Lemonade and reciting poetry by Warsan Shire. The entire performance is an ode to motherhood, and she was introduced by her own mother, Tina Lawson. Jay Z and Blue Ivy cheered her on in the front row.

Getty Images

During her acceptance speech for Best Urban Contemporary Album, she mentioned her hopes and dreams for her family. "It's important to me to show images to my children that reflect their beauty so they can grow up in a world where they look in the mirror, first through their own families, as well as the news, the Super Bowl, the Olympics, the White House and the Grammys, and see themselves," she said. "And have no doubt that they're beautiful, intelligent, and capable."

Getty Images

February 23, 2017

Beyoncé cancels her scheduled headlining performance at the Coachella music festival. In a statement, she said she was "following the advice of her doctors to keep a less rigorous schedule." She instead will perform in 2018, and Lady Gaga performed in her place at 2017's festival.

February 25, 2017

Though they don't attend the Oscars, Beyoncé and Jay Z do attend a pre-Oscars party to celebrate the movie Lion. Bey wore a body-hugging dress by Nicole Miller and posted photos to her website after the party.

Instagram / Beyonce

March 2, 2017

Jay Z and Beyoncé take Blue Ivy to the Los Angeles premiere of Beauty and the Beast. All three wore Gucci, and Blue Ivy wore a dress with a "25" patch, which may have been a nod to Adele, who had recently said Beyoncé deserved her Album of the Year Grammy.

Beyonce.com

March 7, 2017

To celebrate stepdad Richard Lawson's 70th birthday, Beyoncé attends a party wearing a figure-hugging black velvet dress and thigh-high boots.

Beyonce.com

A week later, she posts photos from the big event on her website. Some fans noted she was wearing the same earrings as she did in the music video for "If I Were a Boy," perhaps signaling she was having twin boys.

April 2, 2017

Bey posts a slew of Instagram photos of her date night with Jay Z, including one incredibly sweet shot of Blue Ivy hugging her pregnant belly.

April 4, 2017

Beyoncé releases a new song and music video on Tidal. The video for the piano ballad "Die With You" features footage from her and Jay Z's wedding, the birth of Blue Ivy and her current pregnancy with twins. She also releases a playlist of her favorite love songs.

April 16, 2017

Instead of performing at Coachella as scheduled, Bey has a low-key Easter celebration with family. Later, she shares a compilation of photos set to "Lovely Day" by Bill Withers.

April 22, 2017

Beyoncé reunites with Destiny's Child bandmates Kelly Rowland and Michelle Williams to promote Rowland's new book, Whoa Baby!, which is all about pregnancy and motherhood.

April 28, 2017

Jessica Alba throws a Michael Jackson and Prince-themed party, and Beyoncé is one of the guests. Nearly a month after the party, Bey posts "throwback" photos of the event, showing off an Any Old Iron jacket that is literally named after her.

April 29, 2017

Beyoncé's mother, Tina Lawson, throws the inaugural Wearable Art Gala at the California African American Museum. Bey attends the gala and wears an elaborate floral headdress. "Mom, you inspire us all to be generous, selfless, positive, to have empathy, to love and to praise creativity in the world," she wrote on Instagram. "It brought tears to my eyes to be a witness to what you and Richard have created together."

April 30, 2017

Jay Z and Beyoncé go to an L.A. Clippers game. Later, Bey shares Instagram photos of the $7,560 outfit she wore to the game.

May 4, 2017

Beyoncé's rep issues a strongly worded statement after a gossip blog claims she's had lip injections. “What do you know about the effects of pregnancy on a woman’s entire body? Please tell me," Yvette Noel-Schure wrote. "I stood silent during Beyoncé’s first pregnancy when you thought it was okay to bully her like the cowards you are, when you accused her of never being pregnant, but I simply cannot this time.”

May 4, 2017

Beyoncé posts photos of herself wearing a shirt featuring the word "Preggers." The shirt was only $23 from Asos, and it sold out almost immediately.

May 14, 2017

For Mother's Day, Beyoncé, Blue Ivy, Jay Z and Tina visit the Museum of Ice Cream in Los Angeles. Beyoncé and Blue Ivy wear matching Dolce & Gabbana dresses. Beyoncé shares photos on Instagram a week later.

May 20, 2017

Her friends and family throw her a Carter Push Party, with guests like Serena Williams, La La Anthony and Destiny's Child bandmates Michelle Williams and Kelly Rowland. The next day, Bey posts artistic portraits from the celebration, featuring Jay Z.

May 30, 2017

The day after Memorial Day, Bey posts a sweet photo of herself holding Blue Ivy in a swimsuit. It's the last thing she posts on Instagram for weeks.

June 16, 2017

News breaks that the twins were born earlier in the week. There were some reports Beyoncé went into labor June 13, but there was no confirmation on the exact birth date, names and sexes of the twins. However, some speculated she had a boy and a girl based on this photo of a visitor outside of the LA hospital with a card labeled "B & J."
