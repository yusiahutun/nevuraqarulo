Toradora! ending explained: What happened between Ryuuji and Taiga?

June 30, 2024

Toradora quickly became popular and loved by fans everywhere. The romantic-comedy series, written by Takemiya Yuyuko and illustrated by Yasu, is about two high school students, Ryuuji and Taiga. They are determined to date each other’s best friends. But as they spend time together, they have a change of heart which influences their end game. So, what does the Toradora! ending mean for the two?

Some animations make the heart warm, and Toradora is one of them. It was first released as a light novel and became one of the most popular romantic animations. It aired from 2008 – 2009 and released 25 episodes, including some special episodes at the end. However, the anime's ending confused many viewers; most don't know what happened between the main characters.

How did the Toradora manga end?

The Toradora manga plot is from a light novel. But in the series, the original story took to a turn and had a different ending to the one planned by the author. The anime ends on high school graduation day when the main characters have their final meeting. It's here that Taiga made her ultimate decision to leave Ryuji.

However, some viewers think the Toradora manga depicted the separation story better than the animation. In the manga, the illustrator beautifully traced the extended panels, and the grief of separation was precisely expressed.

Why did Taiga leave Ryuuji at the end?

Taiga leaving Ryuuji completes her character arc. Taiga was a mess at the start, and Ryuuji helped her learn how to interact with others. That's without being condescending, violent, and rude because of her broken upbringing and wrecked home life.

By the end of the anime, Taiga has matured a lot. In the last two episodes, she watches how Ryuuji inadvertently damages his home life and fixes it in a matter of days. This makes Taiga decide that, at this point, she must also fix her issues.

Does Toradora have a sad ending?

The Toradora ending is a bit complicated. In the entire series, Taiga and Ryuuji have a lot of issues. Taiga is from a well-off family; however, her parents divorce, and her father gets a new wife. Taiga is put aside by her dad, who cares more about his new wife. She realizes this quickly and asks to stay in her place, which neighbors Ryuuji's place.

Taiga is adversely affected by her family situation – though she doesn’t accept this at the beginning of the series. It makes her impulsive, rude, and argumentative at the start of the animation. Her character development arc is the main element of Toradora's anime. She realizes she needs to change to be a better lover for Ryuuji.

But contrary to popular belief, the ending wasn't sad; it was satisfying and rounded the main anime characters' arcs well.

Did Ryuuji and Taiga get married?

In the anime, unfortunately, Taiga and Ryuuji didn't marry. They gave up on marriage after realizing they couldn't do it without their family and friends.
